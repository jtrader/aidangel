# Kanku anantjanya

Funnel-web munu mouse spider kanku anantjanya palyanu walytja kuka (snake) kanku anantja uwantjamaaltu tjunkunytjaku. Redback kanku anantjanya ice pack-angka palyanu — ura tjunkunytjaku wiya. Kulila pulka ngaranytjala medical aid-ku nintitjaku. 

## Funnel-web kutjara mouse spider kutjara

Katja ngaratjunu - **pressure immobilisation** (ngarala [snake bite](/kb/snake-bite)) munu **000**-ku walatjura. 

## Redback spider

1. Ice pack (kalyatjunu) tjunkunytjaku, tjuni tjunkunytjaku. 
2. **Wiya** pressure bandage tjunkunytjaku – ngarala katja ngaratjura pulka ngaratjunu. 
3. Medical aid-ku nintitjaku. Antivenom ngarala ngaranytjala tjunkunytjaku. 

## White-tail, huntsman, walytja Australia kanku anantja

1. Kanku anantja tjunkunytjaku soap munu kapi tjunkunytjaku. 
2. Ice pack tjunkunytjaku. 
3. Medical aid-ku nintitjaku kulila pulka ngaranytjala, kanku anantja tjunkunytjaku, kutjara kanku anantja kutjara kutjara tjunkunytjaku, munu katja kutjara tjunkunytjaku.
