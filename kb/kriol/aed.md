# AED — Automated External Defibrillator

An AED is a machine wot sambala imapusa ontop a main enjin fiinijin wot mekem de hert blak. Modern AEDs enjiniyat fo imapusa enjin—stadjit, attachim de pets, an imapusa de main enjin. An AED is a machine wot sambala imapusa ontop a main enjin fiinijin wot mekem de hert blak.

## Wot wan AED i du

Wan Automated External Defibrillator (AED) i lukim de hat fidim, an if i paindim wan fidim wot kyan bi shok (ventricular fibrillation o pulseless ventricular tachycardia), i gibit wan kontrol shok fo traim mekit de hat fidim go bak long olrait.

## Hou yu yusim wan AED

1. **Onimim.** Opinim de kaba o presim de pabutan.
2. **Pudim de pets** long de bos blo de porsin, jas laik yu luk long de pets pakij:
   - **Adult:** wan pet blo de rait klaibon, wan pet long de lep said blo de armpit.
   - **Pikinini wea i blo andaet yia o blo anda 25 kg:** yusim pikinini pets if yu garra—wan long de senta blo de bos, wan long de midul blo de baksaet.
3. **Stan klia** wail de AED i lukim de hat fidim.
4. **Gibim de shok** if de masin i teldim yu—mekshua no garrabit nobodi i totjim de porsin, den presim de flajing batn.
5. **Gohedim CPR instanli** afta de shok o if de masin i no teldim yu fo gibim shok. De AED main enjin i lukim ebri tu minits.

## Speshal taimting

- **Wut sait skin o ren:** draiim de bos kwiktaim an gohed. Muwbe fram potihol.
- **Heri bos:** de pets mas totjim de skin—sheivim o pulum de pets kwiktaim if yu nidim (mos kit garra wan reja).
- **Mashin wea i jimin laek peismeika:** pudim de pet etlis et sentimita farawe fram eni samting wea yu kyan luk.
- **Medisin petjis:** tekimaot an waipim de said bifo yu pudim de pet.

## Paindim wan AED

Yusim de [GoodSAM AED Painda](https://www.goodsamapp.org/locatorMap) o ringim **000**—de opis saet i kyan shawim yu de klosap AED wea i bin rejista.

## Luk tu

Wan AED i de step **D** blo [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd). Gohedim [CPR](/kb/cpr) wail de masin i bin kolekt an pudim.
