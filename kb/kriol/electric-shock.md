# Mainja Laigit

Na bin touchim di pashin until di powa op. Wans sife, followim DRSABCD, singgaut 000, kikim wun an mainja shok. Ifin di pashin luk olrait afta mainja laigit, di pashin garra goospidal kauntam.

## Foswan sife

- **Na bin touchim di pashin until di powa op.** Di laigit kaiya bin gobru an gobru yu.
- Finishim di powa daidrait o puluda owtan di makain.
- Longa haiy-vollej powa lain (e.g. jamp daun waiya), **stap ailis 8 miitawei** an singgaut **000** — onli imorgensii sirvis bin meigim di pleis sife.

## Wans sife

1. Followim [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
2. **Singgaut 000.**
3. Ifin im na ansa an na breth nomal, jidan [CPR](/kb/cpr) an jainim en [AED](/kb/aed).
4. Kulim eni [wun](/kb/burns) anda kol wota runnin ailis 20 miniut — oli moni taim deya **entrins an egzit wun**.
5. Kikim [shok](/kb/shock).
6. Go longa goospidal ebri taim, ifin di pashin luk wela — Insait damij an hat ritim trabul bin gro apta.
