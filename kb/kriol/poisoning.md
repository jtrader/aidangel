# Mek-im Sik Gidap

Sipoja eni brabli sik naur dija poisin, garra singaud langa Poisons Information Centre on 13 11 26. Langtaim dija pashin bin lug-garran eni laif, o dija pashin bin bad wan, garra singaud 000. No garra mek-am spew-garran, samtaim ol bin tok yu.

## Wanem garra mekim

1. Folom [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
2. **Singaud langa Poisons Information Centre on 13 11 26** (24/7, Australia eniwei).
3. Singaud **000** sapos dija pashin bin lug-garran eni laif, hirim bin tharran tharran, fit, o dija pashin bin bad wan.
4. **No garra mek-am spew-garran** sapos Poisons Information Centre bin tok yu speshalwan.
5. Keep-imwanwan lewan sampl dija poisin, gubuldring o spew-garran blanga save-garran.
6. Sapos bin lug-garran eni laif bot bin hirim bin tharran tharran, karr-am langa dija [recovery position](/kb/recovery-position).

## Hirim bin tharran poisins (gas, smok, fium)

- Muwum dija pashin langa frishwan warrum **onli sapos seif** blanga yu mekimdijawan.
- Singaud **000**.
- Los-im eni strongwan blaus.
- Redi blanga stat [CPR](/kb/cpr).

## Karrim poisins (skin)

- Tek-imdaun dija sikwan blaus, langa glaas.
- Wot-im dija skin langa kulwan wota, minijum 20 minij.
- Singaud Poisons o 000.

## Button bateri bin daun-dijawan

Lidl kids bin daun-dijawan button bateri bin **brabli medesin erjensi**. Singaud **000** imijatli — bateris ken burn-garran thru dija throt langa owas.
