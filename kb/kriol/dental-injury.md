# Brukkan-Out Tiit

Wolim wun brukkan-out adult tiit long im hed (crown), washim isili blanga milk o' sol-woda sapos i' gad-gad, en den pudum bek long deya hol (socket) sapos yu kyan — o'taim kipim long milk o' spidul en tekam go langa dentist-man insaid 30 minit.

## Brukkan-out adult tiit

1. **Wolim deya tiit long im hed (white part)** — no ketchim deya rut enitaim.
2. Sapos i' gad-gad, washim isili blanga milk o' sol-woda (no woda sapos yu kyan oboijidim). NO skrubim.
3. Trai blanga **pudim bek long deya hol (socket)** laika im bin jidan pas taim, en meigim deya pashin baitim isili wun klinin klot.
4. Sapos yu kyan't pudim bek, kipim deya tiit long **milk** o' long deya pashin **oun spidul** (bitwiin cheek en gum, sapos i' ol nampala blanga no swolowum).
5. Go lukim wun **dentist-man insaid 30 minit** — mo' kwik yu go, mo' beta.

## Beibi tiit

**No** pudim bek wun brukkan-out beibi tiit — i' kyan meigim nogud deya adult tiit weya i' bin groin. Bilap yu go lukim wun dentist-man.

## Brukkan o' kap-kap tiit

- Kipim eni smaulwan pisiz blanga tiit long milk o' spidul.
- Washim deya maouth isili blanga hot-woda.
- Pudum kowld pak long deya cheek blanga meikim deya swelin go down.
- Go lukim wun dentist-man kwik taim.
