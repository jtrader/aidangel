# DRSABCD Akshun Plan

DRSABCD akshun plan eman Australia Resuscitation Council im seven-step primary survey blanga enijing emergency: Danger, Response, Send for help, Airway, Breathing, CPR, Defibrillation.

## DRSAABCD im minim

DRSABCD (sometime rait olsem D.R.S.A.B.C.D.) eman di oda blanga prayaaratis yu wok garra wen yu ketch wan sikwan o injadwan pipul long Australia. Detwan diich evriwan garra akriditid fos aid kos, en detwan bi di speshalwan blanga evri odawan long jismob noleg beis.

1. **D — Danger.** Chek di ples seif blanga yu, oda pipul en di peshen bifo yu go klozup. Komon deinja jidan trafik, ilektrisiti, faiya, woda, fum en anigri enimals o pipul.
2. **R — Response.** Tok en touch. Ask strongawan *"Yu kan hir mi? Openg yu ai. Skwiz mai han."* Yuz di COWS wei (Kan yu hir mi? Openg yu ai? Wat yu neim? Skwiz mai han).
3. **S — Send for help.** Kol **000** blanga ambulens. Put yu fon long spika so yu garra yu han fri.
4. **A — Airway.** Openg di maut. If dija woda o samting yu kan si stopim, rulum di pipul long said en kliaim. Den tilt di hed bek en liftim di chin blanga openg di eirwei.
5. **B — Breathing.** Luk, hir en fil blanga normalwan brip blanga nomo den **10 seken**. Sometime di snorin brip emi NAIT normal brip.
6. **C — CPR.** If di pipul nomo gija ripains en nomo brip normalwan, stat CPR — **30 ches kompreshen tu 2 reskyu brip** long reit 100-120 kompreshen evri minit.
7. **D — Defibrillation.** Put AED wan taim yu ketchim en bilongi di vois.

## Wan taim blanga yuz DRSABCD

Yuz DRSABCD long eniwan pipul hu luk olsem i bin kolaps, nomo gija tingin, hevi injad o di lebul blanga im ripains yu nomo kan faindim kuiktaim. Detwan di seim wei weda yu dilin garra kadiek arest, dran, ilektrokushon, anafilaksis, hed injuri o nomo gija tingin kolaps.

## Impotin riimainda

- If long eni step di pipul gija ripains en brip normalwan, putum long di [rikavari posishon](/kb/recovery-position) en wachim til di ambulens kamin.
- Yu no katin ches kompreshen blanga mo den 10 seken wan taim.
- If yu wanwan garra beibi o pikinini, gija 2 minit blanga CPR bifo yu libum en kol blanga halp.
