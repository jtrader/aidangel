# Siza en Epilipsi

Taem sambala bin ketsim siza, yu garra protektim, lukat oltaem, en nomo holan o pudum enijing langa olabat maunt. Orta siza, pudum olabat langa rikovri posishin en kipim olabat fain. 

## Taem siza bin kaman

1. Kipim lukat **oltaem** wen siza bin stat.
2. Pulum ol jijing weyawei so dei nomo garra bin injad.
3. Putum wun softwan anda thar hed (jumper, towel).
4. Lubandum thar klothes raun netha nek.
5. **NOMO** holem dum olabat, o pudum enijing langa olabat maunt.
6. Letim siza kaman en go.

## Orta siza

1. Folowim [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
2. Pudum olabat langa [rikovri posishin](/kb/recovery-position).
3. Medjim eni injuris.
4. Mekim olabat fain – dei mait bin konfyus o slipi fo wunwei taem.
5. Dei garra bin stap witimap olabat til dei fain genset.

## Ringim 000 if

- Siza bin stap longgwa den **5 minist**
- Wun sekon siza bin kaman kwikli 
- Sambala bin nomo kaman konshas anda 5 minist
- Dei bin injad, preginent, daibetik, o naimbi olabat fers wan siza
- Siza bin hapand insaid wata

## Fiva siza (pikinini)

Fiva siza kin kaman from fiva, yuwsali langa pikinini 6 muns tu 6 yias.
- Rolim pikinini langa olabat said; nomo holan.
- Tekeimof olta klothes.
- NOMO kulim olabat from sponjing o bading.
- Askim fo medikal halp orta.
