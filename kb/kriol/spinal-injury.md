# Saposbin Hem Krait Long Bakbon (Spinal Injury)

Sapos yu smartim wanwan maintaim spoinjas, weda yu bin foldaun long hait, o kraim motoka, o bin dayvim strong, o sapos yu lukim samwan weya i bin krait fo stadaim bakbon na i no bin ansar, yu mas sapos datwan maintaim i bin krait long bakbon. Yu no garra muvim dijan pesin waila i no bin deya insai enijing weya i bin denja; kipim hed na nek stedi en koalambam 000.

## Yu mas sapos hem krait long bakbon wen

- Yu bin foldaun long hait
- Dayving o serfing aksiden
- Rout trafik krais
- Kras o bigwan krait long bodi
- Eni krait long bodi long pesin weya i no bin sabi enijing

## Saens (long pesin weya i bin sabi enijing)

- Pein long nek o bak
- I no bin pilim enijing, o pilim olsem wanwan sting, o no bin pilim enijing
- I no bin able fo muvim ol han o fut
- I no bin able fo holem urine o shit

## Wanem yu garra dum

1. **Koalambam 000.**
2. Talim dijan pesin **i no garra muv** en deya yu tokim gut deya.
3. Sabaudim dija hed wida ol han long evri said, kipim dija hed long laen wida dija bodi. Yu no garra pulum o tawnim.
4. Sapos dei mas muvim (eg. denja, vomitin, o fo dum CPR), yusum wanwan **log-rol** wida brolada helpa, kipim dija hed, nek en bak streit.
5. Sapos i no bin sabi enijing bot i bin brithin, kearfuli putum long wanwan cheinjim [recovery position](/kb/recovery-position) sabaudim dija hed en nek.
6. Sapos i no bin brithin normal, erwey kaman fas — biginim [CPR](/kb/cpr).
