# Diabetic Emergencies

Sapos wanbala pìpul hu bin geda diabetes deya komfyus, gandan oltaem o sikwan, yu mas biliib se gula blood bin lapta daun (hypo) en gibim kain gulawan miil o wata iif dei kuud swala. Kulum 000 iif dei nomo geda beta, lus deya min, o luk jida wata bin draid ap graibala. 

## Hypoglycaemia (daun gula blood)

**Olabat saen:** sori, gandan oltaem, sheik-sheikwan, angri, komfyus, edek, tok blaim blaim, bebad kain behabya.

**Iif dei bin deya min en kuud swala:**
1. Gibim **gula** — soft drink (nomo diet), juus, jeli biins, gluukoos tablet.
2. Ripiit ebri **15 miniit** jantil dei bin geda beta.
3. Foloumap longawan kaabodraid (senwich, biskiit).
4. Iif nomo imphruumant, **kulum 000**.

**Iif dei nomo bin deya min:** kulum 000, foloum [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd), putum intu [rikabri posishan](/kb/recovery-position). Nomoh gibim eniting bai maus.

## Hyperglycaemia (ap gula blood)

**Olabat saen:** graibala toosti, pii-pii eklan, nojia, phruuti brít, droowsi — stei deya owa auwaz o deis.

- Foloum [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
- Elphim deya insuliin iif dei bin ask.
- **Kulum 000** iif deya kondishan bin mek woda o dei bin stroong droowsi.

## Wen yu bin daawt, gibim gula

Iif yu nomo bin shuur weda it bin a hypo o a hyper, gibim gula. It bin elph a hypo en it nomo bin mek so graibala wor in a shoort taim.
