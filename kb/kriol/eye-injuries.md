# Ai Bin Dat Injuri

Sikwan grog imin go in ai, klinimot det ai garra klin woda otaim seilain. Neiba pulumot samting imin go deip insaid ai, kiba-ap boimob det ai en kolim 000. Kemikal imin go insaid ai niidim plenti woda en ambulens.

## Samting imin go insaid (grog, ailash)

1. Nomoda rabim det ai.
2. Benim heda dumaji det injurid ai imin luk daun.
3. Flasimota garra klin woda, seilain otaim ai-wash solushun.
4. Ifin imin nomo kamot, kiba-ap det ai garra klin ped en tok garra daktur.

## Samting imin go deip insaid ai

1. Nomoda trai tumoda pulumot det grog.
2. Kiba-ap det ai laet garra peipa kap otaim ped — nomoda presham.
3. Kiba-ap det **narra ai tu** tumoda stopim boimob det ai muvin.
4. **Kolim 000.**

## Kemikal Splash

1. **Straetwei** flasimot det ai garra kuil ranin woda atlis **20 minit**. Benim heda dumaji det kemikal imin ran awai fram det gud ai.
2. Holim det ailid opin.
3. **Kolim 000.**
4. Ifin yu sabi det kemikal, dalim det hos bitwain yu en det daktur.

## Blou to det Ai

Putim kuil pek dumaji tumoda rejuisim swelin. Tok garra daktur ifin ijas eni chenj long ai, seba pen, blad insaid ai, otaim ai imin luk difran.
