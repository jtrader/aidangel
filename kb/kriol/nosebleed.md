# Blud garram nus

Sidon raitap, leanub iŋgal, en strongwanba pinjim sohpwan pat blanga nus planga leas 10 miniuts. Lukim dokta sipos blud bin blo miŋgul afta 20 miniuts o sipos im binin afta wan hed injuri.

## Waat wi dalim yu du

1. Mekim dija pesin sidonab en leanum **slaitli iŋgal** (no bakad – blud insaid stumuk mekim yu vomat).
2. **Pinjim sohpwan pat blanga nus** strongwanba, jas bilow dija brij, planga leas **10 miniuts** en no larram go.
3. Putum wan kolwan pak long dija fohed o bak Said blanga nek.
4. Dalim dija pesin briddim truw mouth en spitumat eni blud.
5. Afta 10 miniuts, larram go sohpwanba. Sipo im stil blidin, pinjim planga neda 10 miniuts.

## Lukim dokta sipos

- Blud no stop afta 20 miniuts
- Blud binin afta wan hed injuri
- Dija pesin deikim blud-tinniŋ midekesin
- Hebi bludin mekim dêm fil fain
- Blud garram nus kaman bak aftan en aftan

## Afta blud binin

- No bloim nus planga sam houas.
- No kaikaim hotwan driŋs en no duim hebi werk planga res blanga de.
