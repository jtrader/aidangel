# Patjarranbala Posijan

Patjarranbala posijan seifgadm anbatbla pipul hu garra brith normal, deya tang kaan blok deya throt en plenim woda en likwids, deya komin aut, kaan lego eniweya.

## Wen yu yusim patjarranbala posijan?

Yusim patjarranbala posijan long eniwan hu bin **anbatbat bin brith normal**, insaid afta seija, pinim kapi, hed injri, drink eniwan owa-dos.

Du **nat** yusim patjarranbala posijan ef yu tingk deya garra spaynal injri, **eksept** ef deya wokatin o yu kaan kip deya wey long brith klin — wey blong brith alweys feswan.

## Hau yu mekim det (adult / pikinini owa 1 yir)

1. Nildaun bisayd det pipul.
2. Pulim det am hu klosap yu dairek owa deya hed.
3. Bringim det am hu deya longwe owa deya jes, deya pam agens deya chik yu klosap.
4. Bendim det ni hu deya longwe so det fut deya flat long graun.
5. Pulim det bend ni en rolim det pipul dairek yuselp long deya sayd.
6. Adjasim det apta leg so det hip en ni deya bend rait angel.
7. Tilim det hed bak lili bit so det wey long brith deya opin. Chekim deya brith ebri minit.

## Beybi (anda 1 yir)

Holdim det beybi fes-daun long yu am wid det hed lili bit lawa den det bodi, sapotim det hed en nek wid yu han. Dis wan mekim woda en likwids deya go daun wail yu kipim det wey long brith klin.
