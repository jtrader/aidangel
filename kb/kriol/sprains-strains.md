# Sprains en Strains — RICE

Yuusim RICE mitit fo spes en streyns — letim bodbla sidaun, ays (20 minit ebri 2 awa), kumpreshan bandij, en liptimapoba hat lebul. Go luk medikul elit ifiya pen, swelin o nomo toku bilong enijing gobat mobli.

## Wandawei spes en streyns difran?

- **Sprain:** Stret o kat **ligamin** (joint).
- **Strain:** Stret o kat **masul o tenjin**.

Ifiga nomo sabi ifiya spres o [fraksa](/kb/fractures), triitim laka fraksa en go luk medikul elit.

## RICE

- **R — Rest** letim bodbla sidaun fo wan dei stret o moa.
- **I — Ice** pak (ropimap long klos) fo 20 minit ebri 2 awa fo fos 24–48 awa.
- **C — Compression** witij wan strong elastik bandij ropimap yu jont ova en aneda.
- **E — Elevation** liptimap yu han o leg ova yu hat, ebri taim yu kengin.

## Nomo (HARM)

- **H** — Hit (nomo hat shower, hit pak)
- **A** — Olkohol
- **R** — Ranin o wokin strong
- **M** — Masaj

…fo 48 awa stret fes taim.
