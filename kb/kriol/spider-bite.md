# Spaida Binbaits

Wanba o muws spaida binbait garra bi tritbat langa presha imobelaisan laika sneik binbait. Retbek spaida binbait garra bi tritbat langa aisk peik — nomo benda. Luk medikel elp sapos eni samting luk rongwan.

## Wanba o Muws Spaida

Trit wiya laif-tretningwan — aplai **presha imobelaisan** (luk [sneik binbait](/kb/snake-bite)) en kol **000**.

## Retbek Spaida

1. Aplai wan kold peik (repmabat) fo meikem du im hedawei.
2. Nomo aplai presha bendej — i bin meikem krai heviya.
3. Luk medikel elp. Entivenom deya sapos yu nidim.

## Wait-teilyil, Hantsmen, oda Ostreliyan Spaida

1. Wasim di binbait langa sop en woda.
2. Aplai wan aisk peik.
3. Luk medikel elp sapos symptams wursen, di binbait garra infeckt o di binbait deya langa fes o klostu ai.
