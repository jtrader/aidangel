# Diabetes Ingkerreke

Angkeme nyente diabetes-karlte, ngkweltye-karlte, aremany-karlte, nampe-karlte rrangkaye, yernkerrtye renhe kwaterte ngwerneke intetyame. Angkeme renhe 000 ye ngwerneke intetyame, ngkaartengele atnyenete, ngkartetye.

## Hypoglycaemia (yernkerrtye renhe kwatere wirte)

**Aremanyenge:** aremany, urlate, arenge, kwatye, ngkweltye, ngkweltye-karlte, rlterrte, rrangkaye.

**Angkeme ngkartetye-karlte, kwatere wirte:**
1. Kwatere **wirte** ngwerneke — soft drink (ngkartetye diet-karlte), juice, jelly beans, glucose tablets.
2. Angkeme renhe **15 minutes** ye ngwerneke intetyame.
3. Ngwerneke intetyame arrerne kwatere wirte (sandwich, biscuit).
4. Angkeme renhe ngwerneke, **call 000**.

**Angkeme ngkaartengele atnyenete:** call 000, follow [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd), place in [recovery position](/kb/recovery-position). Do NOT give anything by mouth.

## Hyperglycaemia (yernkerrtye renhe kwatere wirte)

**Aremanyenge:** kwatye, kwatere wirte, ngkweltye, urle-urle, rlterrte — hours or days.

- Follow [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
- Help them with their own insulin if they ask.
- **Call 000** if their condition is worsening or they become drowsy.
