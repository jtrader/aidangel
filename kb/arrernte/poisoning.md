# Apmere ingwe-kenhe ngkwele

Urlampe apmere ingwe-kenhe ngkwele, kaltureme the PME: Poisons Information Centre on 13 11 26. Kenhe ingkerreke apmere akwete aretyenhe, arratye rrangkarra arntarnte ahelhe, kaltureme 000. Ungkwelarenge apmere arntarnte the anangkethe the, ungkwelarenge akerte the apmere ahwele angkwerre. Arratye apmere anngeme the apmerte the ingwe-kenhe ngkwele the kaltureme the angkwe.

## Kaltureme the angkwe

1. Angkwerre [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
2. **Kaltureme the Poisons Information Centre on 13 11 26** (24/7, Australia-wide).
3. Kaltureme **000** kenhe apmere akwete aretyenhe, arratye rrangkarra arntarnte ahelhe, akwete ingkerreke the, arratye rrangkarra arntarnte ahelhe akwete ingkerreke the, arratye rrangkarra arntarnte ahelhe akwete ingkerreke the.
4. **Ungkwelarenge apmere arntarnte the anangkethe the** ungkwelarenge akerte the apmere ahwele angkwerre. Arratye apmere anngeme the apmerte the ingwe-kenhe ngkwele the kaltureme the angkwe.
5. Ingkerreke the apmere ingwe-kenhe ngkwele the, ingkerreke the apmere ingwe-kenhe ngkwele the, ingkerreke the apmere ingwe-kenhe ngkwele the, ingkerreke the apmere ingwe-kenhe ngkwele the.
6. Kenhe apmere akwete aretyenhe, arratye rrangkarra arntarnte ahelhe, place in the [recovery position](/kb/recovery-position).

## Ingwe-kenhe ngkwele (gas, smoke, fumes)

- Angemele the apmere ingwe-kenhe ngkwele the, angkwerre the apmere akwete aretyenhe **only if safe** for you to do so.
- Kaltureme **000**.
- Loosen tight clothing.
- Be ready to start [CPR](/kb/cpr).

## Absorbed poisons (skin)

- Remove contaminated clothing wearing gloves.
- Flood the skin with cool running water for at least 20 minutes.
- Kaltureme Poisons or 000.

## Button battery ingestion

Children swallowing a button battery is a **medical emergency**. Kaltureme **000** immediately — batteries can burn through the oesophagus within hours.
