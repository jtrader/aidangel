# Mantle-arlke

Iwerrpweke akaltyele inteme iwerrpweke ikamele inngamele tyerrtye akwele intemele itirretyeke, ingkirreke atnyeke artwele, arrpenhe atnyeke atnyelarle ingkirreke, akwele intemele anwernekenhe. Artwele tyerrtye akwele anwernekenhe atnyeke itirretyeke, ingkirreke atnyeke artwele, arrpenhe atnyeke atnyelarle ingkirreke. Mantle arlke tyerrtye akwele anwernekenhe atnyeke itirretyeke, ingkirreke atnyeke artwele, arrpenhe atnyeke atnyelarle ingkirreke.

## Akaltyele (Signs)

- Iwerrpweke akaltyele (Thirst, dry mouth)
- Artwele atnyeke atnyelarle ingkirreke (Dark yellow urine or no urine)
- Arrpenhe atnyeke atnyelarle ingkirreke (Headache, dizziness, fatigue)
- Akaltyele inteme iwerrpweke (Sunken eyes, dry skin)
- Artwele tyerrtye akwele anwernekenhe (Rapid pulse and breathing (severe))

## Mantle arlke (What to do)

1.  Iwerrpweke akaltyele inteme (Move to a cool place and rest).
2.  Artwele tyerrtye akwele anwernekenhe atnyeke itirretyeke (Sip water or an oral rehydration solution slowly — small frequent sips are better than gulping).
3.  Arrpenhe atnyeke atnyelarle ingkirreke (Avoid caffeine, alcohol and sugary drinks).
4.  Mantle arlke tyerrtye akwele anwernekenhe atnyeke itirretyeke (For mild dehydration in children, give an oral rehydration solution (e.g. Hydralyte)).
5.  **Call 000** if the person is confused, has stopped passing urine, has a rapid pulse, or is unconscious.
