# Jellyfish-arlke artwele tyerrpenhe kengke, uthene iltyenhe

Bundaberg-eke urlteleke akenhe (Arrernte-werne ingwele akenhe) jellyfish-epenye tyerrpenhe nhanhe, vinegar-erlenge artwele tyerrpenhe nhanhe 30 seconds-eke. Kenhe 000-erlenge ingwele. Bluebottle-epenye uthene akenhe, iltyenhe tyerrpenhe nhanhe, tentacles-ele kngwelye, hot water-erlenge kele iltyenhe tyerrpenhe nhanhe.

## Tropical jellyfish (box jellyfish, Irukandji)

Bundaberg-eke urlteleke akenhe ingwele akenhe, jellyfish-ele tyerrpenhe nhanhe, life-arlke artwele akngwele akenhe.

1. **000-erlenge ingwele.**
2. **Vinegar**-erlenge artwele tyerrpenhe nhanhe 30 seconds-eke.
3. Vinegar-ele arrpenhe ingwele akenhe, tentacles-ele kngwelye (gloves-erlenge artwele, tyerrpenhe nhanhe; fingertips-erlenge artwele, tyerrpenhe nharrke).
4. [CPR](/kb/cpr)-erlenge ingwele — box jellyfish stings-ele cardiac arrest-erlenge artwele akngwele akenhe.

## Bluebottle-arlke uthene non-tropical jellyfish-arlke

1. Seawater-erlenge artwele tyerrpenhe nhanhe (fresh water-erlenge arrpenhe) tentacles-ele kngwelye.
2. **Vinegar**-erlenge arrpenhe.
3. **Hot water**-erlenge (rescuer-ele artwele alharrke akenhe) artwele tyerrpenhe nhanhe 20 minutes-eke. Hot shower-ele artwele.
4. Hot water-erlenge arrpenhe ingwele akenhe, cold pack-erlenge artwele pain-eke.

## Stingray, stonefish, cone shell

**Hot water**-erlenge artwele tyerrpenhe nhanhe 90 minutes-eke. **000**-erlenge ingwele — these stings-ele artwele fatal-eke.

## Blue-ringed octopus

Pressure immobilisation-erlenge artwele (as for [snake bite](/kb/snake-bite)) uthene **000**-erlenge ingwele. CPR-erlenge ingwele — the venom-ele artwele breathing muscles-ele akngwele.
