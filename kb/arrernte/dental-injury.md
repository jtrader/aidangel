# Mape-arlpe arrpenhe-atwelye

Mape-arlpe arrpenhe-atwelye ingkirreke atnyenetyeke, kete alhetyeke. Milk-eke rrake-kitye salt water-eke rrake. Wetyenheke alhetyeke arlturle atwatyeke. Milk-eke rrake-kitye arrpenhe-aelhe atyentetyeke. Dentist-eke alhetyeke 30 minutes-ekarre mpwaretyeke-atwelye.

## Mape-arlpe arrpenhe-atwelye ingkirreke

1. **Mape-arlpe atnyenetyeke crown-eke (arlperle arntirrke-atwelye)** — arrpenhe-aelhe root-eke atnyenetyenhe.
2. Arratye-aelhe-atwelye, milk-eke rrake-kitye salt water-eke kete alhetyeke (water-eke kete alhetyenhe, arntirrke-aelhe-atwelye). Do NOT scrub.
3. Wetyenheke alhetyeke arlturle atwatyeke, arrpenhe-aelhe-atwelye. Atyentetyeke person-eke wetyenheke-atwelye kweltye alhetyeke arntirrke-atwelye cloth-eke.
4. Arntirrke-aelhe-atwelye wetyenheke atturletye-atwelye, **milk-eke rrake-kitye person-eke arrpenhe-aelhe atyentetyeke** (cheek-eke rrake-kitye gum-eke, ingkirreke-aelhe-atwelye, arrpenhe-aelhe-atwelye).
5. See a **dentist within 30 minutes** — arrpenhe-aelhe-atwelye, arrpenhe-aelhe-atwelye.

## Baby teeth

Do **not** wetyenheke atturletye-atwelye baby teeth-eke — arrpenhe-aelhe-atwelye damage-etyeke ingkirreke-atwelye teeth-eke. Still see a dentist.

## Broken or chipped tooth

- Save any tooth fragments in milk or saliva.
- Mouth-eke kete alhetyeke warm water-eke.
- Apply a cold pack to the cheek to reduce swelling.
- See a dentist as soon as possible.
