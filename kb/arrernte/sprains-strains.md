# Sprains nthurr-arlk nthurr-arlk-tyang — RICE

RICE-eke-pwanytyarrentye arrwekelhe-warlpentye nthurr-arlk nthurr-arlk-tyang-kenhe — Alhterretye, Ice (20 minutes akenhe 2 hours arrwekelhe), Compression ngkwenteyartelye, nthurr-arlk etyame-ketye. Arrekwele-werne arntamere-werne arrwekelhe-werne ingkernhe-werne arrwekelhe-werne nthurr-arlk nthurr-arlk-tyang-kenhe.

## Kake-arte-pe arrwekelhe akenhe nthurr-arlk-tyang?

- **Arrwekelhe:** arrwekelhe-warlpentye **ligament** (arltyarre).
- **Nthurr-arlk-tyang:** arrwekelhe-warlpentye **muscle akenhe tendon**.

Nakenhe-ke nakenhe-arte arrwekelhe akenhe [fracture](/kb/fractures)-tyang, fracture-arte arrwekelhe-warlpentye akenhe arntamere-werne arrwekelhe-werne ingkernhe-werne arrwekelhe-werne.

## RICE

- **R — Alhterretye** arrwekelhe-werne akenhe 24 hours-werne.
- **I — Ice** pack (ngkwenteyartelye-werne arrwekelhe-werne) 20 minutes akenhe 2 hours arrwekelhe 24–48 hours-werne.
- **C — Compression** arrwekelhe-werne arrwekelhe-werne ngkwenteyartelye-werne akenhe arrwekelhe-werne.
- **E — Etyame** arrwekelhe-werne arrwekelhe-werne akenhe arrwekelhe-werne.

## Nakenhe-arte (HARM)

- **H** — Heat (akele-arte arrwekelhe, heat packs)
- **A** — Alcohol
- **R** — Arraretye akenhe arrwekelhe-warlpentye
- **M** — Massage
