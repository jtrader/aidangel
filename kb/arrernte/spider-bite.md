# Aherre Artwe-akerte Atweye

Funnel-web arenye me-ipe aherre mpwe-akerte atweye akaltyele-anetyeke, aknanperre atwemele-akerte akaltyele-anetyeke. Redback arenye apayperre arteke atweye, ikwere-le aknanperre atwemele. Aknanperre artetye, ingkitirrele atweye-werne. Medical aid-eke urlale. 

## Funnel-web me-ipe aherre mpwe-akerte

**Pressure immobilisation** ( [snake bite](/kb/snake-bite) ) -eke alkngerre aneme. **000** -eke urlale.

## Redback aherre

1. Atweye-werne aknenpe-akerte (arrpenhe-akere) apayperre itertyele. 
2. **Aknanperre atwemele** - apayperre arteke atwerre.
3. Medical aid-eke urlale. Antivenom -eke urlale.

## White-tail, huntsman, arrpenhe arenye aherre atweye

1. Aherre atweye-werne soap me-ipe kwaterreme-le atwerremele.
2. Apayperre arteke itertyele.
3. Medical aid-eke urlale. Aknanperre arteke atweye, ingkitirrele atweye-werne.
