# Arelhe-artweye ntanke — Urretyele ankwele akantyemele

Angke arelhe-artweye-ke ntanke kake rrangkarra Antartike-werne, 000-ngarrarre keme, rretnye urretyele ankwele akantyemele arlkwenthe-arlkwenthe-werne. Kurne arelhe-artweye arrpenhe akantyemele, arratye rrerne.

## Lyete arenge (arratye artweye nhenge ntanke)

1. **000-ngarrarre keme.**
2. [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd)-ngarrarre arenge.
3. Arelhe-artweye **arratye rrerne** rritnye kngelhele. Mpepe-mpepe angke akantyemele ntante rrangkantye.
4. **Urretyele ankwele akantye**:
   - Ankwele arratye ngkenhe akantyemele (angke-arelye angke akantyemele).
   - Ntanke nhenge arelywerne altyerre, arlkwenthe-arlkwenthe akantyemele – ntwangkarre-ntwangkarre nhenge ankwele arratye akantyemele.
   - **Arlkwenthe-arlkwenthe-werne nhenge akantyemele**, awenyele angke akantyemele.
   - Ntanke nhenge arelywerne ankwele-werne arnterre.
5. Arlkwenthe arratye **akantyemele** nhenge, angke akantyemele.
6. Ntanke-ke arratye tyerrtyerre akantyemele arratye. Ankwele ngkenhe akantyemele.
7. Arrenge arretyeke akantyemele. [CPR](/kb/cpr) alhwerne arntarrke.

## Lyete aherre

- Ntanke nhenge **angke arelye** – ntanke-ke arelye angke akantyemele artweye nhenge angke arelye.
- Angke tyerrtyerre-kerne angke akantyemele, angke arlkwenthe-arlkwenthe akantyemele.
- **Angke akantyemele** ankwele arratye.
- Ankwele **angke arelye** – nhenge rretyele akantyemele arratye nhenge angke rretye.
- **Angke arelye** nhenge artweye nhenge rretye arratye akantyemele.
