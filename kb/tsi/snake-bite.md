# Snake Bite — Pressure Immobilisation

Sopos yu tink wan snake baiit yu long Australia, kol 000 an bandijim da baiit pleis strong-strong, stat long da baiit an go-go op da huul leg o han, den mek-am splint an meik da sikwan stap jast lo wan pleis.

## Wot yu shud du (tink-em snake gat poison)

1. **Kol 000.**
2. Folow [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
3. Meik da sikwan **stap jast lo wan pleis** an laikdaun. Sapos hi o shi muuv, da poison go spreid.
4. Put-em **pressure immobilisation bandage**:
   - Yuus wan bigwan hevi-bandij (o sopos no gat, yuus raun-raun klos, teer-em).
   - Stat lo da baiit pleis, wrem-em strong-strong — jast laik yu bandijim sopos yu sprain.
   - Kaminyuu **go-go op da huul leg o han**, kova-em moos wot yu ken kova-em.
   - Maak-em da baiit pleis lo da ausaid blo da bandij.
5. **Mek-am splint** da leg o han blo-wei so hi o shi no muuv-muuv.
6. Raid-em da taim wey da snake baiit an da taim wey yu put da bandij.
7. Tok gudwan long hi o shi an luk-luk hi o shi. Mek-em redi fo stat [CPR](/kb/cpr).

## No du dizwan

- **No** washm da baiit – da poison wey stap lo skin go help-em aidenifai da snake.
- **No** kat-em o sak-em da wun.
- **No** put-em wan tourniquet.
- **No** teik-em of da bandij – jast paramedic o da pipul long hospitul ken du dizwan.
- **No** trai fo kech o kil-em da snake.
