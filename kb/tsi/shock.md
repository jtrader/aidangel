# Shock (Hypovolaemic / Circulatory)

Shock emi wan krisis wey blood i-drop low. Sain blong shock emi skin i-pael, kol, damp, pulis i-wik mo i-fas, olsem naujia mo konfusion. Lainim daon dispela pikinini, mekim tretmon blong emi, kipim emi hot mo kolim 000.

## Rekonaesim shock

- Skin i-pael, kol, damp
- Pulis i-fas mo i-wik
- Briding i-fas mo i-shallow
- Naujia, thas, dizzines, konfusion
- Ristlisnes, enzaeti o drowsines
- Ivenchuli emi slip daon mo lusim konsasnes

## Wanem blong mekim

1. **Kolim 000.**
2. Folowim [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
3. Lainim daon dispela pikinini — yu mas **no** reisap lek blong emi (karen AFA5 gaidens).
4. Tretim spesis blong shock (olsem, kontrolim [bliding](/kb/bleeding), menijim [bens](/kb/burns) o [anafilaesis](/kb/anaphylaxis)).
5. Lusim klos wey i-tait.
6. Kipim emi hot long blangkit — yu mas **no** putum direk hit long emi.
7. Givim smol sip blong kol wata, sapos emi konsas gud, emi save swollow, mo i-no gat engjuri long bela blong emi.
8. Sapos emi ankonsas o emi gat trabol long briding, putum emi long [rikoveri posisen](/kb/recovery-position).
9. Reashorim emi mo stap lo emi go long taem wey help i-kam.
