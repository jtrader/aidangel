# AED — Automated External Defibrillator

An AED gime wan ilektrik shok blo torunop wan hat weh i bin stop. Ol nupela AED i mekem blo ol pipul weh no bin trening — torunopim, putum ol pats wia i shoum, en foloum ol vois tingting.

## Wey AED i mekem

Wan Automated External Defibrillator (AED) i lukim hat blo pipul en, sapos i lukim wan shokable ritim (ventricular fibrillation o pulseless ventricular tachycardia), i gime wan ilektrik shok blo torunopim wanhat blo kam bek normal.

## Hau blo usim wan AED

1. **Torunopim.** Opinim lid o presim paoa boton.
2. **Putum ol pats** long nek blo pipul wia i shoum long pat pakiging:
   - **Adalt:** wan pat anda long rait kolabon, wan pat long lef said anda long ampit.
   - **Pikinini anda 8 yia o anda 25 kg:** usim paediatrik pats sapos i stap — wan long senta blo nek, wan long baksa blo baksa.
3. **Sanapwei** wataim AED i lukim hat ritim.
4. **Gime shok** sapos i bin telim — mekem no wan pipul i tachingim pipul, den presim flehsing boton.
5. **Torunopim CPR stret** afta shok o sapos no shok i bin telim. Deh AED wul lukim agen evri 2 minit.

## Special sityueshan

- **Whet skin o reen:** draiim nek kwiktaim en toronopim. Muw wei from samting blo wara.
- **Heri nek:** pats mas tachingim skin — shevim o pulum ol pats kwiktaim sapos i bin nid (planti kits i gat wan reza).
- **Implantid peismeika:** putum pat atlis 8 cm wei from eni lukluk langa.
- **Medikeishan paches:** tekemaut en waipim ples bifoa putum pat.

## Painim wan AED

Usim deh [GoodSAM AED Finder](https://www.goodsamapp.org/locatorMap) o kolem **000** — deh opereita ken direct yu long klosap registad AED.

## Luk tu

Wan AED i step **D** blo [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd). Toronopim [CPR](/kb/cpr) wataim deh vais i bin kolek en putum.
