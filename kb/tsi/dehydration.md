# Sik bin drai

Muvum daurla oen kain kol ples en aloum i sip wata oen oeral rehaidreshon solushon eslo. Strong drai — i nogat brein, nomo i yurin, i hart-bit i ran esmaet — i nidim ambulans.

## Sain

- Oen drai maute, i tirsti
- Strong yalo yurin o nomo yurin
- Hedek, ol i gira gira, i hauf silip
- I ai i go insaed, i skin i bin drai
- I hart-bit en i bret i ron esmaet (strongwan)

## Wat fo mekem

1. Muvum i go oen kol ples an i rest.
2. Sipim wata oen oeral rehaidreshon solushon eslo — plenti likl sips i beta den sapraesim.
3. Nomo kafi, alkohel en switi dringk.
4. Fo likl drai fo pikinini, giwim oen oeral rehaidreshon solushon (eg. Hydralyte).
5. **Kol 000** sapos daurla i nogat brein, i bin stop yurin, i hart-bit i ran esmaet, o daurla i silip olgeda.
