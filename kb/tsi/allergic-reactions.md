# Slight to Mediam Allergic Reactions

Suppose allergic reaction 'em no too bad — skin rash, lips or face swelni, thorni ﬁlin — teikim dawe wate make-em, give-im antihistamines suppose medicine blong hem, an luk-luk gud suppose im make-em wos o’im gowin anaphylaxis.

## Sign blong slight to mediam

- Swelni a’ lip, face o’ eye
- Skin rash (bilap jimpl jimpl an krachi) o’ rashes
- Thorni ﬁlin a’ maoth
- Bel pein, vomit (bug bite allergy)

## Wate for do

1. Movem dawe a’ pipal frem wate make-em suppose yu sabi. Pulim dawe a’ bi sting suppose yu kow, by flekem em saide-wez.
2. Stay wit' em an luk-luk gud suppose im make-em wos o’im gowin [anaphylaxis](/kb/anaphylaxis).
3. Give-im antihistamines onli suppose preskribe for dat pipal.
4. Suppose a’ pipal gat wan akshan plan, foloim.
5. **Kallim 000 instea** suppose yu kow some hadwei for breth, tang o’ throt swelni, throt pein, haf kof, foldaon o’ gidi — tretım laka anaphylaxis an yuzim adrenaline.

Pipal hu sabi dey gat wos allergy AN asthma shud alwez yuz im **adrenaline autoinjector fast**, den im asthma riliever.
