# Hat Aitak

Ol saen blanga hat aitak iusli i stat long jes pen o presa, plaba taim i spretdan long ja, am o bak. Kal 000 instret, sidaunim da pesin, lusum lafap, en gibin aspirin 300 mg if da pesin no alergik.

## Lukim hat aitak saen

- Pen long jes, presa, taitnes, hevi o skwisim
- Pen i spretdan long ja, nek, am, bak o beli
- Bres sot
- Kaka pen, swit, gira-gira hed, o litul hed i go daun
- Skin blanga da pesin i wait o kold en wet

Disla ol saen ken no klia long ol oman, ol elda pipul en ol pipul hu gat daibitis — wen yu no klia, kal 000.

## Wanim fo du

1. **Kal 000.** No letim da pesin draib deirselp.
2. Halpim dem sidaun long wan gudfala posisin (plaba taim haf-sidaun, ni bend).
3. Lusum tait lafap.
4. Gibin **300 mg blanga aspirin** (wan adalt tablet o 4 x 75–100 mg) fo tyu, eksip if dem alergik, bledin hevi, o bin tokim dem nomo fo tekim aspirin.
5. If dem gat angina medisin (GTN spre o tablet), halpim dem yusum it.
6. Mekim dem fil beta en lukim dem gud.
7. If dem no tok en no bres nomali, stat [CPR](/kb/cpr) en putim [AED](/kb/aed) apta it kamin instret.

## Hat aitak genst kadiek arest

Wan **hat aitak** emi bloz pat blanga bodi blanga hat — da pesin iusli i mebi tok. Wan **kadiek arest** emi wen da hat i stap — da pesin i go daun en no bres nomali. Kadiek arest nidim CPR en AED instret.
