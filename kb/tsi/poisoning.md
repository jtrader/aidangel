# Poisoning (Garbage-Garbage)

Sopos yu tinkim samwan bin kaikai poison, yu garra ringim Poisons Information Centre long 13 11 26. Sapos datwan pikinini i no save gerap, o yu tink im i sik tumas, yu garra ringim 000. No mekim im traim blong vomitim, longtaim olsem deitwan imin tokim yu.

## Wanem blong mekim?

1. Folloim [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
2. **Ringim Poisons Information Centre long 13 11 26** (24/7, Australia-wide).
3. Ringim **000** sapos datwan pikinini i no save gerap, o imi putim imselp long ol samting blong pulim win, o imi gerap o imi sik tumas.
4. **NO mekim im traim blong vomitim** longtaim olsem deitwan imin tokim yu long Poisons Information Centre.
5. Holim wan sampl blong poison, kontainer, o vomit blong ol savim wanem kain bait i stap.
6. Sapos imi no save gerap bot imi pulim win, putim im long [recovery position](/kb/recovery-position).

## Pulim win poison (gas, smok, fumes)

- Kukim datwan pikinini go long er we i klin **longtaim olsem i sef** for yu blong mekim datwan.
- Ringim **000**.
- Pinisim ol klos we i taet.
- Ridi blong statim [CPR](/kb/cpr).

## Absorbed poison (skin)

- Tekin daunim ol klos we i gat poison long taim yu werim ol gloves.
- Wasim datwan skin wetim kol wara blong 20 minit o mor.
- Ringim Poisons o 000.

## Kaikai botten battry

Ol pikinini we i kaikai wan botten battry i wan **medikal emergensy**. Ringim **000** stretwe — ol battry inap bonim diswan osefagus longtaim olsem tu awa.
