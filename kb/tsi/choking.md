# Choking — Bigwan, Pikin en Bebi

Sopos samwan him murit, him no kowm, him no toktok o him no bret, gib 5 shap bak blow insaid imda solda bone, den 5 tes trast, en jensim jensim gwen gwen so untel imda wey blo bret him klea o garram dei him lusim wain blo laif.

## Lukaut blo Choking

- **Naismur (kowm nais):** imda pesin him stil kowm, toktok o bret. Enkorijim dei blo kowm kowm go.
- **Hadmuri (kowm nogut):** kowm kwaet, no kemp toktok o bret, holim imda nek, imda lip blo dei him jens blo blu. Mekem miting miting.

## Bigwan o pikin (wan yia o mo)

1. **Kompala 000** (o gibim samwan noda wan blo kompala).
2. Bendim imda pesin forwod en gib ap tu **5 shap bak blow** insaid imda solda bone wit imda hil blo yu han. Sekim imda mout afta ebri wan.
3. Sopos imda samting him no klea yet, gib ap tu **5 tes trast** — sem han pozisin olsem CPR bat slowa en shapa.
4. **Jensim jensim** 5 bak blow en 5 tes trast.
5. Sopos garram dei him **lusim wain blo laif**, putum daon klea klea insaid imda graon en statim [CPR](/kb/cpr).

## Bebi (ande wan yia)

1. **Kompala 000.**
2. Putim imda bebi fesdaon alang yu am, het daon daona imda tes, sapotim imda het en jo.
3. Gib ap tu **5 stog bak blow** insaid imda solda bone.
4. Ranum imda bebi fesap en gib ap tu **5 tes trast** wit 2 finga insaid imda daona tes bone.
5. Jensim jensim go go untel imda wey blo bret him klea o imda bebi him no kemp muw — den statim bebi CPR.

## Nomo mekem

- Nomo putum yu finga blainli insaid imda mout — yu kemp pushim imda samting go fara wei moa.
- Nomo mekem abdaminal trast (Heimlich) — Australian Resuscitation Council gaijlain dei yusim bak blow en tes trast.
