# Seizures an Epilepsy

Long taim wanpela seizure i stap, yumi mas protektim datpela man o oman from injury, taimim, an neva daunim dem o putim samting insaid dem mout. Afta dat, putim dem long recovery position an tokim dem olraite.

## Long taim wanpela seizure i stap

1. Lukim **taim** weh datpela seizure stat.
2. Pulim ol kainkain samting iweit for protektim datpela man o oman from injury.
3. Putim soft samting (jumper, towel) andahed long dem.
4. Loosem tait clot blong dem raun long nek blong dem.
5. **No** go daunim dem, holdim dem daun, o putim samting insaid dem mout.
6. Mekim seizure i go on til em pinis.

## Afta datpela seizure

1. Folowim [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
2. Putim dem long [recovery position](/kb/recovery-position).
3. Managem ol kainkain injury.
4. Tokim dem olraite — dem maet konfyus o drusy for sam taim.
5. Stap witim dem til dem olraite gen.

## Kolim 000 if

- Datpela seizure i stap longgata den **5 minutes**
- Wanpela sekon seizure i folowim kwiktaim
- Datpela man o oman no kam bek consous long 5 minutes
- Dem gat injury, pregnet, diabetic, o em fes taim blong dem for gat seizure
- Datpela seizure i hapen long wata

## Febrile convulsions (pikininni)

From fiva, usyuli long pikininni 6 mans go tu 6 yias.
- Rolim datpela pikininni long saed blong dem; no daunim dem.
- Pulim iweit ol kainkain ol clod weh i extra.
- No kolem dem blan spongim o wasim dem.
- Sikim medikol help afta dat.
