# Hypothermia

Hypothermia em i taim body blo man em i kamkol daun long 35°C. Muwim da person go insaid shelter, tekem wet klos blo dem orait, hitim dem slo-sulo wit blanket an body hit, an kol 000 sipos em i hevi.

## Sain

- Skin i shekim-shekim (em mait stop if em i hevi tumas)
- Skin i kol, i waet
- Num (no filim)
- Tok blo dem i slared, dem i giris, dem i laik slip
- Pulse an brethin slo an no strong

## Waat yu mas du

1. **Kol 000** sipos em i hevi tumas.
2. Muwim dem go long wan ples we i wom, i drai, an i gat shelter.
3. Hanlim dem slo-sulo — if yu muwim dem heva-heva em mait mekim haat blo dem stop.
4. Tekem wet klos blo dem orait; putum drai klos or blanket orait.
5. Kavam hed an nek blo dem.
6. Womim dem **slo-sulo** wit blanket, body wom an wan wom (noken hot) envarment.
7. Givim **wom swit dring** onli if dem i fuli conscient. **Noken alcohol o caffeine.**
8. Noken rabim skin o pusam streit hit (hot wata botol, hita).
9. Redi fo stat [CPR](/kb/cpr) — duwim fo langgar den normal taim in hypothermia.
