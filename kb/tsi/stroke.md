# Stroke — The FAST Test

FAST test fo' stroke — Face wek, Arms wek, Speech humbug, Time fo' ring 000. Ev'ry minit impo'tant; mekim da samwan slipdaung, jingara witim hed liklbit high, an no gib'em enijing throug da mouth.

## Da FAST test

- **F — Face.** Em's face wekdaung wan side? Em kyan smile street? 
- **A — Arms.** Em kyan jingara both arm an hol'em? 
- **S — Speech.** Em's tok humbug? Em kyan undastand yu? 
- **T — Time.** Time em brain — **ring 000 straitchit!**

Ada sain kyan bi sudden numbnes o weknes, sudden kyonfyuson, sudden no kyan si, hed ache too bad, dizzy o no kyan balans.

## Enti yu kyan du

1. **Ring 000** — lukaum wen da siknes did stat; dis kyan affek da tripmen. 
2. Hok-ap [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
3. Mekim da samwan slipdaung witim em's hed an sholda liklbit jingara.
4. **No gib'em enijing fo' kaikai o dring** — maybi em kyan no gulum propali.
5. Mekim em fiil beta an stap witim em.
6. If em go unconscious but em still briiding, put'em long [recovery position](/kb/recovery-position).
7. If em stop briiding stret, stat [CPR](/kb/cpr).

## TIA (liklbit stroke)

If da siknes go wekkwai, maybi em bin transhent iskemik atak. Still ring 000 — em em wanin sain fo' big stroke.
