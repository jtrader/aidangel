# DRSABCD Action Plan

DRSABCD action plan em i Australian Resuscitation Council im seven-step primary survey blanga evri emergency: Danger, Response, Send for help, Airway, Breathing, CPR, Defibrillation.

## Wagenem DRSABCD sing out blanga

DRSABCD (samtaim i rait olsem D.R.S.A.B.C.D.) em i order blanga wanem yu shud mekem fest taim wen yu lukim wan man sik o injur insaet Australia. Em i tich insaet evri akreditid fest aid kurs na em i bakgrap blanga evri narra protokol insaet dis wol nalesh beis.

1. **D — Danger.** Chek mekem ples em i seif blanga yu, bystander na pesen bifor yu garram em. Koman denja em i: trafik, ilektrik, faiya, wata, fium na enimals o pipol wea i agreiiv.
2. **R — Response.** Tok na tasim. Aksim big mouth *"Yu kyan hirrim me? Opin yu ai. Skwiizim ma han."* Yusim ol COWS aprowch (Yu kyan hirrim me? Opin yu ai. Yu neim em wanem? Skwiizim ma han).
3. **S — Send for help.** Kolim **000** blanga ambulens. Putim yu fon long spika so yu kyan kipim yu han fri.
4. **A — Airway.** Opinim mouth. If igat wata o wan samting wea yu kyan lukim wea i blokim, ronim pesen long wan said na kilrim em. Den bendim ed bak na laptim chin ap blanga opinim erwei.
5. **B — Breathing.** Lukim, hirrim na pilhawan blanga normal bredin blanga no mor den **10 sekens**. Sambala taim, gasps em i NO normal bredin.
6. **C — CPR.** If em i no rispon na no bredin normal, statim CPR — **30 ches kompreshen long 2 reskiu bred** long reit 100–120 kompreshen evri minit.
7. **D — Defibrillation.** Putim wan AED kwik taim wen wan igat na foloim vois promps.

## Wen blanga yusim DRSABCD

Yusim DRSABCD long eni pesen wea i lukim olsem i bin kolaps, ankonsonshus, injur hevi, o hu wan lebyl blanga rispons yu kyan't mekim ap kwik taim. Em i di seim freimwok weda yu diil wit wan kadiak arest, drowndin, ilektrokushon, anafilaksis, ed injuri o anoun kolaps.

## Hapi wan ting wea yu mas rimemba

- If long eni step di pesen i rispon na di bredin normal, putim em insaet [rikovari posishon](/kb/recovery-position) na lukim antil ambulens i kam.
- No stopim ches kompreshen blanga mor den 10 sekens long wan taim.
- If yu wanwan wit wan pikinini o smel wan, givim 2 minit blanga CPR bifor yu liiv blanga kolim help.
