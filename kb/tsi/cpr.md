# CPR — Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation

CPR hem kapin long eniwan hu blo anresponsiv an nomat singaut normal. Yu garra yusim 30 chest compressions long 2 breaths long 100–120 compressions evri minit, wulya kapin di depth an mek-em di teknis long bigpela pipol, pikinini an bebi.

## Wen di taim for stat CPR

Statim CPR stretwen di pipol blo **anresponsiv an nomat singaut normal**. Nogat wait — evri minit we yu nomat du CPR, di chans for liv blo kapin go daun long 10%.

## Bigpela pipol an pikinini (ova wan yia)

1. Larim di pipol flat daun long strongpela graun an nildaun long sait blanga di chest blanga dempla.
2. Putim di hil blanga wan han long **daunpela hafp blanga di sternum** (midul blanga di chest). Putim di ada han blanga yu long top an jainim ya fingga.
3. Holim ya ams stret an ya soldas stret ova di han blanga yu.
4. Pushim daun **wan tad blanga di dip blanga di chest** (klozbout 5 sm long wan bigpela pipol).
5. Compressim long wan reit blanga **100–120 evri minit** — klozbout 2 evri seken.
6. Givim **30 compressions**, den **2 rescue breaths**.
7. Keepim 30:2 til di pipol ketch bek, wan ambulans kyam, wan AED telim yu for stop, o yu ken nomat du eni mor.

## Bebi (anda wan yia)

1. Putim di bebi long wan strongpela graun.
2. Yusim **tu fingga** long daunpela hafp blanga di sternum.
3. Compressim wan tad blanga di chest dip long 100–120 evri minit.
4. For breaths, kapa **both di mawth an noz** wit di mawth blanga yu an givim sof-sof puf — jast inaf for luk di chest blo go ap.
5. Keepim 30:2.

## Compression-only CPR

If yu nomat ebul o yu nomat laik for givim rescue breaths, du **kuntinyuwus chest compressions** long 100–120 evri minit. Compression-only CPR blo beta tumas dan nomat du CPR atol.

## Wen di taim for stop

Stopim CPR onli If:
- Di pipol stat singaut normal.
- Wan paramedic o dokta tekova.
- Wan AED telim yu for stap kweit (stat bak stret If dempla telim yu).
- Yu blo taiad tumas an nobodi ken tekova.

## Luuk olso

CPR blo step **C** blanga [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd). Putim wan [AED](/kb/aed) stretwen i kyam — nomat stop CPR for waitim em.
