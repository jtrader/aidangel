# Hem i napat long Spinal Injury

Yu mas ting ‘hem i gat spinal injury’ sapos hem i foldan long hait, rot kraish, daiving insidan, o samwan we nōla kīmiap afta wan aksidan. No muv langa desfella pipol wīnyu tekea hem i save ded. Kipim het en nek hol langa wan sai, en kali 000.

## Yu ken singk we wan spinal injury i bin napan wen

- Hem i bin foldan long hait
- Daiving o sefing aksidan
- Rot trafikk kraish
- Krash o bigfella blunt trauma
- Eni ankonshas trauma peshent

## Saens (long wan peshent we i ashō)

- Pen long nek o bak
- Namnaas, tingglin o los long sensashon
- I nōla muv hans o legs
- Los long bladara o bowel kontrol

## Wat yu musta du

1. **Kali 000.**
2. Talim desfella pipol **no muv** en mekso desfo pipol i rili guta. Holim desfo pipol han long tu said, kipim desfo pipol hed we i laenap long desfo pipol bodi. No pul o twist.
3. Sapos yu mas muv desfo pipol (eg. ded, vomitin, o du CPR), yusum **log-rol** wetda plenti pipol, kipim desfo pipol hed, nek en bak algn.
4. Sapos desfo pipol i ankonshas bat brethin, kiasli putum long wan modifai [rikovri posishon](/kb/recovery-position) holin desfo pipol hed en nek.
5. Sapos desfo pipol i no brethin gudin, erwe i olwe kom fasta — statim [CPR](/kb/cpr).
