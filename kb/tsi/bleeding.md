# Bikpla Blud I Kaman Autsaid

Stopim bikpla blud from kaman autsaid long mekim strong, stretpela, na kipat presha long da wound, slipim da pesin daun, na mekim tritmint wonda shock wail da ambulans i kaman.

## Kwikta mekim

1. **Kallim 000** wonda bikpla blud.
2. Laitim [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
3. Slipim da pesin daun na openim da wound (katim da klos s'po nidim).
4. Mekim **strong, stretpela, kipat presha** wonda yu hand, wan klin klot, o wan pad.
5. S'po blud i stop, holim da presha wonda wan strong banij.
6. Mekim kipat presha — yu **nomata** liftim da dresin wonda chekim.
7. Mekim tritmint wonda [shock](/kb/shock) na kipim da pesin waam.

## Ting we i stap insait

Yu **nomata** pulim autim wan ting we i stap insait da wound. Mekim presha long sait sait blanga da ting na putim moa pad raunim da ting wonda nomata muvim bifo banijim.

## Katim wan paat blanga da bodi

- Kontrolim blud wonda stretpela presha.
- Rapim da katim paat wonda wan klin drai klot, putim insait wan plastik bag, den putim da bag long ais — **nomata long ais stret** o long wata.
- Senim long hospital wonda da pesin.

## Tourniquet

Wan seli-meik tourniquet shud onli yuzim wonda laif-tretnim blud long han o leg we yu nomata kontolim wonda stretpela presha. Mekim 5–7 cm long ap blanga da wound (nomata long oba wan join), taitim wonda blud i stop na makim da taim.
