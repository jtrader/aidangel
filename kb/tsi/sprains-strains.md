# Sprains an Strains — RICE

Yuusim di RICE method blo sprains an strains — Rest, Ice (20 minutes ebri 2 hours), Compression bandage, an Elevation ova di haat lebul. Luk fo medical help if di pen, swelin o loss blo ting mait stil go on.

## Wanim sprain vs strain?

- **Sprain:** stretchim o teerim **ligament** (jont).
- **Strain:** stretchim o teerim **masul o tendon**.

If yu nogat shoo wanim, sprain o [fracture](/kb/fractures), triitim langa olsem wan fracture an luk fo medical sapot.

## RICE

- **R — Rest** di injud area fo atlist 24 owa.
- **I — Ice** pak (rapim insaid wan klot) fo 20 minit ebri 2 owa fo di fos 24–48 owa.
- **C — Compression** wit wan strong elasik banij weya kaba di jont ova an daon.
- **E — Elevation** blo di leg o han owa di haat lebul eni taim yu sabe.

## Noblo (HARM)

- **H** — Hiit (noblo hot shawa, hiit paks)
- **A** — Alcohol
- **R** — Ranim or strong aktiviti
- **M** — Masaj

…fo atlist di fos 48 owa.
