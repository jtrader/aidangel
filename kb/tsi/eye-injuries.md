# Ai Bin Datwan

Sapos samting bin go insaid yuroman ai, washim da-wey wit klin wata o sam sikan wata. No karem da samting we bin go insaid yuroman ai, karem da samting we bin go insaid yuroman ai — kaba-ap tupela ai en kol 000. Kemikal we bin splash go insaid yuroman ai nid 20 minit blo ranin wata en wan ambulens.

## Sam samting blo ausaid (grit, aeilash)

1. Nomore rubim da ai.
2. Tiltim da hed so da ai we bin datwan i daun.
3. Flashim wit klin wata, sikan wata o ai-wash solushon.
4. Sapos i nomore kamaan, kaba-ap da ai wit wan klin pad en lukim medikal dipatmen.

## Samting we bin go insaid

1. Nomore **trai** fo karem da-wey.
2. Kaba-ap da ai slo-slo wit wan peipa kap o pad — nomore presha.
3. Kaba-ap da **nara ai tu** fo stopim tupela ai fo muv.
4. **Kol 000.**

## Kemikal bin splash

1. **Straetwei** flashim da ai wit kol ranin wata fo pas blo **20 minit**. Tiltim da hed so da kemikal i ran-awey fram da ai we i nomore bin datwan.
2. Holem da ailids opan.
3. **Kol 000.**
4. Sapos yu save da kemikal, karem da konteina wit yu go long hosbetal.

## Bin hit long ai

Aplai wan kol pak fo redusem da swelin. Lukim medikal dipatmen sapos i gat eni chenj long lukluk, strong pen, blad insaid da ai, o da ai i luk narapela.
