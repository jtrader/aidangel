# Asthma Attack — 4 x 4 x 4 Plan

Setta da pipul strate, giwi 4 paf brom da blu riliva paffa trua spesa (wan paf wan taim, 4 breditc iitc), wet 4 minit, rimemit iip da pipul no beta. Kol 000 iip da pipul no beta.

## Lukaut fo asthma attack

- Witcin, hostaim kaf, tait tcais
- Hard fo toktok hol sentens
- Worri, luk wait, switit skin
- Blu lip, olgeta kras - kol **000 kwiktaim**

## Asthma Fest Aid Plan (4 x 4 x 4)

1. **Setta da pipul strate.** Pasens en mekam fil gud.
2. Giwi **4 difren paf** brom da blu/grei riliva (e.g. Ventolin, Asmol) — wan paf kwiktaim, tektc **4 breditc** brom da spesa afta iitc paf. Yu'us spesa iip yu garram.
3. **Wet 4 minit.**
4. Iip daiya no beta, giwi neda **4 paf**.
5. Iip daiya stil no beta, **kol 000** en kipa giwi 4 paf iibri 4 minit bipo da ambulens kam.

## Big attack — kol 000 fest

Kol 000 kwiktaim iip:
- Da pipul hagat fo bredt.
- Dia lip luk blu.
- Daiya don kras daun.
- No garram riliva.

Taiya yu wet fo ambulens, kipa giwi 4 paf iibri 4 minit.

## Adrenalin (anafiliaksis witc asthma)

Iip da pipul garram tuu EpiPen en yu no daiya garram big aladji, giwi da **adrenalin fest**, den da asthma riliva. Luk [anafiliaksis](/kb/anaphylaxis).
