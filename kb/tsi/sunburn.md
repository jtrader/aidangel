# Sanban

Muvaut langa san, putum kol wata insaid da eria long 20 minit, putum aafta-san jel, dring plenti wata, an neva burstim blista. Lukim medikal sevis long taim bilong nogud sanban o blista bilong pikinini.

## Wanem fo mekim

1. Muvaut langa san.
2. Kolim da skin we olgeta sanban insaid da **kol ronin wata long 20 minit**.
3. Putum aafta-san jel, aelo vera o som kain lotion bilong da moisturais.
4. Dring plenti wata fo da bodi tek wata gen.
5. Kavam garram bifo go bak auwtsaid.
6. **No burstim olgen blista.**

## Lukim medikal sevis if:

- Da sanban e kavam big eria
- Plenti blista e kakamap
- Da pikinini, smol pikinini o olman/oloman.
- Dei e garram saen bilong [nogud hit bilong bodi](/kb/heat-illness) — big hit, vomit, het e ron, tingting e ronwed.
