# Ḏäṯthun dätthaŋu ga Bäymbirryun

Waṉaŋuŋa dhäyilan miyalkthun märranhamirr yothun. Nhe dhu ga märranhany yothuny dhäwum dätthun. Bitjan nhäthiny, bala nhe dhu ga guḻku'yun ga dharrayun ga ŋurruŋu dhiyaŋuny dätthun ga yukurra nhäman yothun, ga dhäyilan nhäŋun. Yo, nhe dhu ga djämany yothun bitjan nhäŋun, ga djälthuny ga djuḻkmaran ga biyam. Ga nhe dhu ga djämany ga ŋarrakuŋ ga dhäwum ga waŋa 000.

## Dhäruk yothuyŋu

- Yothu balanda — ga ŋurruŋu bitjan ŋayathiny
- Dätthaŋu ganymirr, baybirryun, yukurra nhäman yothun
- Galŋaŋa djuḻkmaran
- Gapu yothu ga ŋäthiny yothu
- Yothu balanda ga ŋurruŋu nhäman
- Gapu yolŋu ga ŋäthiny dätthaŋu
- Yothu balanda ga ŋurruŋu djälthuny

Nhäŋun ga ŋurruŋu — **waŋa 000**.

## Nhäyu dhu djäma

1. Märranhany [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
2. Waṉaŋuŋa dhäyiŋun [spinal injury](/kb/spinal-injury). Goŋdhuny ga guḻku'yun ga dharrayun.
3. **Yothu balanda:** dhiyala ga nhäŋun ga guḻku'yun ga dharrayun. Ga guḻku'yun ga dharrayun ga ŋurruŋu dätthaŋu.
4. **Yothu balanda ga garrka'yun:** dhiyala ga nhäŋun, ga gaḏukthun ga yothu ga guḻku'yun ga dharrayun.
5. Ga biyam ga djälthuny ga djiwuythu ga ŋarrakuŋu.
6. Yalalaŋumirrnydja ga yothu ga ŋarrakuŋ ga nhäman dhäwum ga waŋuny.

## Bäymbirryun

Yothu balanda ga ŋurruŋu ga djälthuny ga ŋarrakala ga guḻku'yun ga dharrayun. Ga yolŋu ga yukurra nhäman yothun ga warrayŋu. Ga ŋayathiny ga yothu ga garrka'yun. Gänal warrayŋu ga dhiyaŋuy yolŋuy ga yothu ga garrka'yun ga yukurra nhäman yothun ga warrayŋu. Warrayŋu ga yothu ga garrka'yun ga 24–48 waŋaŋu ga garrawurra'yun.
