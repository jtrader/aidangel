# Gaṉarrwangu ga Dhambuluk ga djali'yun — RICE

Gaṉarrwanguwun ga dhambuluwuy, dhuwal RICE method - Djäma bäyŋu, bukumirri ŋal'yun (20 minutes bitjan bili ŋuli marŋgithirri every 2 hours), ga gurtha yukurra nhina ŋulkurru gumurrŋura. Dhiyaku ŋäthinyarawuy dhuwal ganyim'mirri ŋuli nhäma biyapul waŋa ŋula nhä dhika ŋuli yolŋu yarrandan dhiyaŋu ŋayaŋu ga dhäkay-ŋula.

## Nhä RICE dhuwal?

- **R — Djäma bäyŋu** dhuwal ŋuli dhambuluŋura marŋgithirri wanhawuy dhipuŋuru 24 hours.
- **I — Bukumirri ŋal'yun** (ŋuli dhuwal bathilaŋun dhiyaŋu gali'ŋura) 20 minutes bitjan bili ŋuli marŋgithirri every 2 hours dhiyakun 24–48 hours.
- **C — Gumurr-ŋamathirri** ŋuli dhuwal djäl ŋuli garrwi'yunarawuy bathila'ŋura, ga dhuwal ŋuli dhambuluŋura ŋurrkuŋu ga dhuwal gumurr'ŋura.
- **E — Gurtha yukurra nhina** dhuwal ŋuli gurtha ŋulkurru gumurrŋura ŋuli bitjan bili ŋuli bukmak.

## Yaka dhika (HARM)

- **H** — Ŋunhi yaka ŋaŋ'yun (yaka gurra-ŋawulil'yun, yaka miyamay)
- **A** — Alcohol
- **R** — Dhunapaŋu ga ŋamakuluyun
- **M** — Buḻŋuŋu

...dhiyakun 48 hours.
