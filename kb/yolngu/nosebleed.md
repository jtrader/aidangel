# Ŋuḻu' ga‐dhäy‐ḏaw'yun

Guḻku'‐gaŋi, rirrakay‐ŋuriŋi, ga wälu‐yiŋmiriŋina dhuŋana dharpa'‐ŋuḻu'‐ŋuriŋina ḏur'yun yaka‐yakaŋa 10 minitjtja. Gaŋa'lili garrkuḻukthin walalaŋgu ŋamathaŋa gurruṯu'mirriw walalaŋguŋ mirriwuŋ dhuŋana biyapul 20 minitjtja ga yaka‐yakaŋa ḏaw'yun, ga yaka‐yakaŋa ŋuliŋuru dhäy‐ŋupanmirriw.

## Nhä walal Dhuŋana gurruṯu'mirriw

1. Nhäŋa ŋathaŋirriw ga dhunupaḻi' ga rirrakay‐ŋuriŋi **dhunupaḻi'** (yaka guyaŋi‐ŋuriŋi — raŋimiriw miyalkŋu dhuŋana dharpa'‐yu'mirriw).
2. **Dhuŋana ḏur'yun dharpa'‐ŋuḻu'‐ŋuriŋina**, dhunupaḻi' ŋuliŋuru biyapul‐ŋuḻu'‐ŋuriŋina, yaka‐yakaŋa **10 minitjtja** yaka bilina.
3. Walalaŋguŋa garrkuḻukthin ŋamathaŋa miyalkŋu walalaŋguŋ miyirr'‐ŋupanmirriwuŋ ga dharaŋgi'‐ŋuḻu'‐ŋuriŋina.
4. Nhäŋa ŋathaŋirriw dhuŋana ŋäŋ'thuŋi dharpa'‐ŋuriŋina ga ŋathili‐ŋäŋ'thuŋi dharpa'‐yu'mirriwuŋ.
5. Ga biyapul 10 minitjtja, bilina ŋamathaŋa. Ŋuli dhuŋana ḏaw'yun biyapul, dhuŋana ḏur'yun ŋulaŋi 10 minitjtja.

## Gaŋa'lili garrkuḻukthin walalaŋgu ŋamathaŋa gurruṯu'mirriw ŋuli yaka‐yakaŋa:

- Yaka dhuŋana ḏaw'yun biyapul 20 minitjtja
- Dhuŋana ŋuliŋuru dhäy‐ŋupanmirriw
- Nhäŋa ŋathaŋirriw dhuŋana guwal dharpa'‐ŋuriŋina dhuŋana räy‐ŋupanmirriwuŋ
- Galki dhuŋana dharpa'‐yu'mirriw dhuŋana ŋathaŋirriw yaka gänaŋgumi
- Yaka dhuŋana dharpa'‐yu'mirriw biyapul‐ŋuḻu'‐ŋuriŋina

## Biyapul dharpa'‐yu'mirriw

- Yaka dhuŋana ŋäŋ'thuŋi dharpa'‐ŋuriŋina yaka‐yakaŋa gaŋa'lili ŋayatham.
- Yaka dhuŋana dhapi'‐ŋayathirri walalaŋguŋ ga ŋayathirri walalaŋguŋ miyalkŋu walalaŋguŋ walalaŋguŋ miyalkŋu walalaŋguŋ walalaŋguŋ giyala‐ŋupanmirriwuŋ.
