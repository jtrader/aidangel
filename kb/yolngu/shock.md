# Buku-lili' (yuṯu'yun ga walŋa-manapan)

Buku-lili' dhuwal' mirithirri rom yukurra ga walŋa-maranhamirri. Dhuwal' rom wiyinmirri ga djarrpi'mirri dhäwu' ga buku-lili' rumbalŋuru. Romnydja ga waŋa yaka walŋa-wuyaŋi, ga gaŋgag ga baḻarrawuy. Ga dhawar'yun romnydja ga waŋa yaka walŋa-wuyaŋi, ga gaŋga ga baḻarrawuy. Ga lili'yun rumbalnydja, ga mirithirri ga buku-lili' maḻŋ'maran. Ga dhawar'yun romnydja ga waŋa yaka walŋa-wuyaŋi, ga gaŋga ga baḻarrawuy. Ga lili'yun walŋa-manapan. Ga dhawar'yun romnydja ga waŋa yaka walŋa-wuyaŋi, ga gaŋga ga baḻarrawuy.

## Buku-lili' maḻŋ'maran

- Yaka walŋa-wuyaŋi, waŋanhamirri ga dhawar'yun walŋana.
- Dhawar'yun yaka walŋana, ga lili'yun rumbalnydja.
- Dhawar'yun yaka walŋana, ga lili'yun rumbalnydja, ga mirithirri ga buku-lili' maḻŋ'maran.
- Dhawar'yun yaka walŋana, ga lili'yun rumbalnydja, ga mirithirri ga buku-lili' maḻŋ'maran, ga mirithirri ga baḻarrawuy.
- Dhawar'yun romnydja ga waŋa yaka walŋa-wuyaŋi, ga gaŋga ga baḻarrawuy.
- Dhawar'yun romnydja ga waŋa yaka walŋa-wuyaŋi, ga gaŋga ga baḻarrawuy, ga lili'yun rumbalnydja.
