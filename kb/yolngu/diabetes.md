# Djarrayarryun Ga Wunmarama Dhiyala Ngatha Yindumirriŋuwuy

Nuli yindumirriŋu walal dhuwala ngathawuy yolŋu yan bili dhu djarrayarryun, gapu dhu baḻarrawuy, ga wuray walalaŋgu dhu waŋanhamirri, wiripuŋuyin dhu bitjan ngathawuy dhuwala nhanŋu gapu dhu dhuḻwunirri yan bili (hypo), ga dhu maḻŋ'marama nhanŋu dhuwal ngathanhamirriny ngatha ga gapu dhu marrtji mayali'mirri yana. Ga dhu nhanŋu gaḻgaŋa 000 nuli nhanŋu dhu nhunhi gapu dhu dhuḻwunirri, ga nuli dhu nhanŋu yuṯayŋu dhu nhanŋu miṯtjiyan, ga nuli dhu nhanŋu walal dhu miṯtjiyan.

## Hypoglycaemia (ngatha miṯtjiyan)

**Dambana'ŋura yan bili:** waŋa ga waŋa, baḻarrawuy, waŋa ga waŋa, ngatha gaŋgarrŋura, djarrayarryun, ŋayaŋu dhuḻwunirri, gulmirri, rumbal dhu rrambaŋin rumbal. 

**Nuli nhanŋu ŋayaŋu dhu miṯtjiyan ga nuli nhanŋu dhu miṯtjiyan:**
1. Ga dhu nhanŋu **ŋatha** -- soft drink (ngatha maḻŋ'marama), juice, jelly beans, glucose tablets.
2. Ga dhu wiripuŋuy marrtji **yan bili** ga dhu nhanŋu dhuwal dhu miṯtjiyan.
3. Ga dhu wiripuŋuy marrtji wiripuŋuŋu (sandwich, biscuit).
4. Ga dhu wiripuŋuy marrtji 000 nuli nhanŋu dhu nhanŋu dhuwal dhu miṯtjiyan.

**Nuli nhanŋu ŋayaŋu dhu miṯtjiyan:** ga dhu nhanŋu gaḻgaŋa 000, ga dhu wiripuŋuy marrtji [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd), ga dhu wiripuŋuy marrtji [recovery position](/kb/recovery-position). Ga dhu wiripuŋuy marrtji nhanŋu dhu nhanŋu dhuwal dhu miṯtjiyan.

## Hyperglycaemia (ngatha gaŋgarrŋura)

**Dambana'ŋura yan bili:** ŋayaŋu gaŋgarrŋura, gurruŋu dhu marrtji mayali'mirri yan bili, ŋayaŋu dhu marrtji wiripuŋuyŋumu, rumbal dhu dhuḻwunirri, rumbal dhu dhärri -- wiripuŋuy marrtji marrtji rom ga rom.

- Ga dhu wiripuŋuy marrtji [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
- Ga dhu nhanŋu wiripuŋuy marrtji wiripuŋuŋu insulin nuli nhanŋu dhu nhanŋu dhuwal dhu miṯtjiyan.
- **Ga dhu nhanŋu gaḻgaŋa 000** nuli nhanŋu dhu nhanŋu dhuwal dhu miṯtjiyan, ga nuli dhu nhanŋu dhuwal dhu miṯtjiyan.

## Nuli nhanŋu dhu nhanŋu dhuwal dhu miṯtjiyan, ga dhu nhanŋu wiripuŋuy marrtji ŋatha

Nuli nhanŋu dhu nhanŋu dhuwal dhu miṯtjiyan, ga dhu wiripuŋuy marrtji ŋatha. Ga dhu nhanŋu dhuwal dhu miṯtjiyan ga dhu wiripuŋuy marrtji ngatha, ga nuli dhu nhanŋu dhuwal dhu miṯtjiyan, ga dhu wiripuŋuy marrtji ngatha maḻŋ'marama.
