# Djälk Yolŋuŋuŋu walŋa, ga dhäkay-ŋulaŋuŋu djuluŋ'ŋumu

Hypothermia dhuwal' walŋaŋuru dhäkay-ŋulaŋuŋuru djuluŋ'ŋumu mala, waŋganyŋura yolŋuŋuru gap'ŋura, dhärra walŋaŋuru balanda miyali'ŋura 35°C warrwam'ŋura. Märrŋuru yolŋu walalanha djarraṯawun'ŋurana, djälk walŋaŋuru djuḻkŋura, ga balanda djämamirri walŋaŋuru dhurrmukŋura ga gap'ŋura, ga buku-ḻung'marama 000 walalaŋgu dhulŋuŋura.

## Yolŋuŋuŋu dhäkay-ŋulaŋuŋu

- Maŋguŋdhurr (walŋaŋuru djälkŋura ga dhärraŋuru maŋguŋdhurr)
- Djuluŋ'ŋumu, dhuluŋ'ŋumu walŋa
- Walŋaŋuru dhäkay-ŋulaŋuŋu
- Dhä-wuyaŋara, dhäkay-ŋulaŋuŋu, ga djuluŋ'ŋumu waŋa
- Djuluŋ'ŋumu, ga dhäkay-ŋulaŋuŋu dhäkay-ŋulaŋuŋu

## Dhäkay-ŋulaŋuŋu walalaŋgu

1. **Buku-ḻung'marama 000** walalaŋgu dhulŋuŋura.
2. Märrŋuru djarraṯawun'ŋurana, ga djuluŋ'ŋumu djarraṯawun'ŋurana.
3. Djälk walalaŋgu, ga dhäkay-ŋulaŋuŋu walŋaŋuru yolŋuŋuŋu.
4. Djälk walŋaŋuru djuḻkŋura; ga balanda djämamirri walŋaŋuru dhurrmukŋura ga gap'ŋura.
5. Djälk walŋaŋuru gap'ŋura ga waŋganyŋuru.
6. Balanda djämamirri **ga dhärraŋuru** walŋaŋuru dhurrmukŋura, ga gap'ŋura, ga djuluŋ'ŋumu djarraṯawun'ŋurana.
7. Dhäkay-ŋulaŋuŋu **djuluŋ'ŋumu dhuḻkŋumu walŋa** ga yolŋuŋuŋu walŋaŋuru. **Gaŋguy wuy walŋaŋuru dhuḻkŋumu ga dhurrkun'ŋumu.**
8. Gaŋguy wuy walŋaŋuru dhuḻkŋumu ga dhurrkun'ŋumu.
9. Dhäkay-ŋulaŋuŋu CPR — walŋaŋuru dhäkay-ŋulaŋuŋu.
