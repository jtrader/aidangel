## Yaka dhuḻwuy‐ŋupan 000

- Muḻkurr ga ḻuku dhäwa' buku‐lakaraŋ 20‐cent coin.
- Muḻkurr ga ḻuku gay'‐ŋuraŋuru, ga garra'‐ŋuraŋuru, ga dhäwa'‐ŋuraŋuru, ga yaka maŋana' nhämaŋ dhuḻwuy‐ŋupan.
- Muḻkurr ga ḻuku gupa'yun ŋäthin, ga ŋula nhä dhäwa' ga yaka maŋana' nhämaŋ dhuḻwuy‐ŋupan.
- Electrical ga Chemical.
- Inhalation (Djambu'‐ŋupaŋuru).

## Yaka dhuḻwuy‐ŋupan.

- Yaka dhuḻwuy‐ŋupan **lotion**, **ointment**, **butter**, **toothpaste** ga **ice**.
- Yaka dhuḻwuy‐ŋupan **blister**.
- Yaka dhuḻwuy‐ŋupan dharpa' ŋayathinyarawuy‐ŋuraŋuru.

## Chemical muḻkurr ga ḻuku

Guŋga'yun Chemical ga dhäwa' dhäwa'‐ŋuraŋuru, bala djarraŋgurrmiyaŋ dhuḻwuy‐marrmarrŋura gurruṯu'‐ŋupan 20 minitthuŋ. Djäma dhuḻwuy‐ŋupan gaŋga'yun.

## Electrical muḻkurr ga ḻuku

Waṉa power source ga dhäwa' dhäwa'‐ŋuraŋuru. Muḻkurr ga ḻuku gaŋga'yun ga dhäwa' dhäwa'‐ŋuraŋuru, bala djäma medical assessment.
