# Gaḻaŋgurr

Waṉa galkirrina walalaŋgawuy ŋayi yaka dhunupana, yurru weyinnha gali'ŋuru. Bima ŋayi yaka ŋäthin, ga yaka dhalwarra, ga yuwalkthi galumbirrina, djäma CPR muka — ganaŋ'thun 000 yulŋu walalaŋgawuynha malaŋuwuy gaḻaŋgurr, ga ŋayi dhuwali walala yaka ŋuli bitjandja walala yuwalkthi djalkthun.

## Bitjanna dhuwalana djämawuy

1. **Ganaŋ'thun 000** yulŋu walalaŋgawuynha malaŋuwuy gaḻaŋgurr.
2. Waṉana galkirrina walalaŋgawuy gali'ŋuru — **yaka nguy gaŋgathirri djunupa gaŋgathirri**. Wäwurkthirri marrtji warraŋul, ga wäyin dhuwali watharr walala gumurr weyinnha, bima dhuwali walala yaka ŋuli dhunupana djämawuy ŋuli gaŋgathirri.
3. Waṉana [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
4. Djarrpi walalaŋgawuy gali'ŋuru, ga bima dhuwali walala yaka ŋuli dhunupana dhalwarra, djäma [CPR](/kb/cpr) muka — weyinnha **5 initial rescue breaths** djuḻk, ga bima walala yaka ŋuli djämawuy djalkthun.
5. Djarrpi walalaŋgawuy gali'ŋuru, ga bima dhuwali walala yaka ŋuli djämawuy gumurr walalaŋgawuy ŋuli goḻkthirri.
6. Djäma CPR ŋayi dhuwali gumurr walalaŋgawuy ŋuli dhunupana djalkthun, ga bima dhuwali ŋayi yaka ŋuli djämawuy djalkthun, ga gumurr walalaŋgawuy ŋuli walalaŋgawuy ŋuli dhunupana djalkthun.
7. **Yaka** nguy yarrpanna gumurr walalaŋgawuy ŋuli walalaŋgawuy ŋuli goḻkthirri yulŋu bitjandja ŋuli walalaŋgawuy ŋuli goḻkthirri.

## Yolŋu walalaŋgawuy gaḻaŋgurr

Bima dhuwali walala yaka ŋuli djalkthun, ga bima dhuwali walala yaka ŋuli djalkthun, ga gumurr walalaŋgawuy ŋuli walalaŋgawuy ŋuli dhunupana djalkthun, yurr dhuwali walalaŋgawuy gaḻaŋgurr ŋuli ŋayi yaka ŋuli dhunupana gaŋgathirri yulŋu walalaŋgawuy gaḻaŋgurr. Gumurr walalaŋgawuy dhuwali walala yaka ŋuli dhunupana gaŋgathirri yulŋu walalaŋgawuy gaḻaŋgurr.

## Maḻgawuy

Bima dhuwali walala yaka ŋuli djämawuy djuḻk, ga bima dhuwali walala yaka ŋuli djalkthun, ga gumurr walalaŋgawuy ŋuli walalaŋgawuy ŋuli dhunupana djalkthun, yurr dhuwali walalaŋgawuy gaḻaŋgurr ŋuli ŋayi yaka ŋuli dhunupana gaŋgathirri yulŋu walalaŋgawuy gaḻaŋgurr. Gumurr walalaŋgawuy dhuwali walala yaka ŋuli dhunupana gaŋgathirri yulŋu walalaŋgawuy gaḻaŋgurr. Djäma CPR ŋayi dhuwali gumurr walalaŋgawuy ŋuli dhunupana djalkthun, ga bima dhuwali ŋayi yaka ŋuli djämawuy djalkthun, ga gumurr walalaŋgawuy ŋuli walalaŋgawuy ŋuli dhunupana djalkthun.
