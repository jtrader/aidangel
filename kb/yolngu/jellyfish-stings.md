# Jellyfish ga Gapuŋuru Dhalwak Mirriny'tja

Yolŋubuynha jellyfish'lili (Bundaberg-ŋuru djiḻk watak), gapu-dhuḏi'nharaŋuy galŋa-djulŋa dhalwakmirriny'tja bala-rriŋi 30 mitjinaŋurruŋu, ga wurrpan 000 wädhun. Bluebottle ga buŋgan nhayilili dhalwakmirriny'nha, ŋuruŋu marrtjin tentacle'nhana yäkuŋu dhambaraŋala ga yindi-dhawar'yuna bakthunmaraŋala gapu'lili.

## Yolŋubuynha jellyfish (box jellyfish, Irukandji)

Bundaberg-ŋuru djiḻk-watak, nhini dhalwakmirriny'tja w'aŋganydja jellyfish-buyŋu djarraŋgu ga nhini nhanukalaŋuwuy walŋa-nyamir'yuna.

1. **000 wädhun.**
2. **Vinegar** dhuḏi'nharaŋu bala-rriŋi galŋa-djulŋa dhalwakmirriny'tja ngurruŋu 30 mitjinaŋuruŋu.
3. Waŋiŋi vinegar, tentacle'na ŋuruŋu dhambaraŋa (glove-thun'tja ŋunuŋuru ga djäma; ŋunuŋuru ga djäma ga waŋganyŋuru baḏak bitjarra).
4. Rrupiya yan nhini [CPR](/kb/cpr) djämaŋu — box jellyfish dhalwakmirriny'tja ga märr gi ŋayaŋu-ḏarrpi'yun.

## Bluebottle ga buŋgan ŋulaŋuruŋu jellyfish

1. Gapuy ga djäl-guyaŋa garrwapurruŋuru (baḏak ŋulaŋuruŋu gapuy) tentacle'na yäkuŋu dhambaraŋala.
2. **Waŋiŋi** vinegar ga djäma.
3. Gi ŋunuŋuru gapu'lili **bakthunmaraŋa** (ŋunuŋuru ga dhuŋgarr' yindi ŋuli ga ŋatha-nyamir'yuna) 20 mitjinaŋuruŋu. Dhuŋgarr'tja gi shower-thun'tja djämawuy.
4. Waŋiŋi hot water, ga cold pack-thun'tja ŋay' ga djäma rirrakay-dhawar'yunmaraŋala.

## Stingray, stonefish, cone shell

Gi ŋunuŋuru gapu'lili **bakthunmaraŋa** 90 mitjinaŋuruŋu. **000** wädhun — nhini dhalwakmirriny'tja walŋa-nyamir'yunaŋawuy.

## Blue-ringed octopus

Pressure immobilisation djämaŋa (bitjarra [snake bite](/kb/snake-bite)) ga **000** wädhun. Rrupiya yan nhini CPR djämaŋu — ŋäŋ'tjukuwuy ga gi wulwulŋuru guyaŋanhawuy-nyamir'yuna.
