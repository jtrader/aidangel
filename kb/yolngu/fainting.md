# Gaŋguyunaraw (Syncope)

Gaŋguyunaraw yeku malany ŋuriŋi dhuŋanaŋaŋu malana dharrayun. Marrtji rrupiya dharapul mirriyarraŋ’ ga nhina galki dhupurr’k. Djalkirra marrtji yana nhe ga mulkanha ga nhini nhe dharrpirraŋ’ ga walgaŋ-dhun nhe mulkanha.

## Dhäkay-ŋäma dharrpiŋa

- Dhuŋanaŋaŋu goŋmirri marrtji gunganharra ga djalkirra dhupurr’k
- Birraŋdhurru ga goŋmirri djalkirra gunganharra ga dhupurr’k
- Marrtji dharrpiŋa goŋmirri dharpumirr ga nhina galki dhupurr’k
- Birraŋdhurru ga goŋmirri dharrpiŋa dhupurr’k

## Nyiŋi dhuŋanaŋaŋu?

1. Goŋdhun [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
2. Yaka gäkarraŋ ga nhe dharrpirraŋ’ ga nhe djalkirraŋ’ ga marrtji mulkanha ga nhini nhe dharrpirraŋ’ ga walgaŋ-dhun nhe mulkanha. Ga nhe birraŋdhurru.
3. Walgaŋ-dhun nhe mulkanha ga nhini nhe dharrpirraŋ’ ga walgaŋ-dhun nhe mulkanha.
4. Mulkanha nhe ga nhini nhe dharrpirraŋ’ ga walgaŋ-dhun nhe mulkanha.
5. Ga nhe mulkanha nhe ga nhini nhe dharrpirraŋ’ ga walgaŋ-dhun nhe mulkanha.
6. Muldhurr nhe ga nhini nhe dharrpirraŋ’ ga walgaŋ-dhun nhe mulkanha.
7. Guŋgaŋnha nhe mulkanha ga nhini galki dhupurr [recovery position](/kb/recovery-position).
