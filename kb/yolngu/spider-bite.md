# Ḏäḻwu' Yolŋu Matha

Funnel-web ga mouse spider bitethun wapan waṉa’yu yurr pressure immobilisationnha gänaŋura yakan ga nhäŋini bitenha. Redback bitesthun yakan bitenha ŋarraku ice packnhan – yaka luku bandagenhaŋ. Yaka ŋuli ŋayi yulŋun nhäyiny’tja mala ŋula nhäyi bitenha.

## Funnel-web ga mouse spider bitethun

Walkurrhin bitenha djakana wal’ŋu ḏaŋŋa wal’ŋu – apply **pressure immobilisation** (nhäma [snake bite](/kb/snake-bite)) ga waŋa **000**.

## Redback spider bitethun

1. Apply cold pack (wrappedthun) ŋarraku djorranha.
2. Yaka apply pressure bandagenhaŋ – ŋayi yaka djorranhaŋ.
3. Walŋan nhärra’ medical aidnhan. Antivenom waŋgany’thu ŋayi ŋuli wangun.

## White-tail, huntsman, ga Australiaŋ’ malanha spider

1. Waŋgany’thu bitenha ŋarraku soap ga waṯaŋ.
2. Apply ice packnhan.
3. Walkurrhin yaka medical aidnhan ŋuli ŋayi yaka ŋurr’yun bitenha, ŋuli ŋayi yaka ŋorr’yun bitenha, ga ŋuli ŋayi yaka ŋorr’yun bitenha ŋarraku ŋayaŋuŋ’ ga ŋuli ŋayi yaka ŋorr’yun bitenha ŋarraku ŋula nhäyiŋ’.
