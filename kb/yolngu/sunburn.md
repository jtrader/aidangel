# Djälma

Gaḻa yukurra ŋamathaŋa, goŋmirri yukurra marrtji gaḻaŋura, djuḻkuluŋu wata yukurra nhäma 20 minitthu, dhuḏi'yunna djaṉaŋ gaḻaŋuru djämamirri, gapu djarr'yunna ḏumurru', yaka dhurrwaraŋganana djalma. Yakaŋuru djalma gurrupan warray gaṉaŋ'maraŋga'warrŋga, liŋgu yukurra nhäma rumbalwuy gaṉaŋ'maraŋga'warrŋga walalaŋgu waḻkura'walalŋga'wuy.

## Nhä yukurra nhäma

1. Gaḻa yukurra ŋamathaŋa, goŋmirri yukurra marrtji.
2. Goŋmirri yukurra marrtji gaḻaŋura, **djuḻkuluŋu wata yukurra nhäma 20 minitthu**.
3. Dhuḏi'yunna djaṉaŋ gaḻaŋuru djämamirri, alo vera, ga yukurra nhäma dhunupa.
4. Gapu djarr'yunna ḏumurru' djamarrkuli'na.
5. Dhuḻwutthunna yolŋu yukurra nhäma gali'ŋura.
6. **Yaka djalma gurrupan warray.**

## Yakaŋuru djalma gurrupan warray gaṉaŋ'maraŋga'warrŋga

- Djalma ŋuriŋi yukurra nhäma ḏumurru'puy mala
- Djalma yukurra nhäma dhurrwaraŋganana mala
- Yolŋu yukurra nhäma waḻkura'yu, gäma'yu, ga dhungatj
- Djalma yukurra nhäma rumbalwuy [dhal'yunna](/kb/heat-illness) — ḏumurru'puy dhal'yunna, rumbalwuy, rrambaŋa, yaka nhäma warray
