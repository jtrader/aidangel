# Djälkthurr-dharrana ga Djälkthurr-yätj

Djälkthurr-dharrana dhu ga-waynha goŋmirri djawuluŋnha, goŋmirri djawuluŋnha, ga dhu ga-waynha djunnguŋnha garwuraŋ'nha. Djälkthurr-yätj (heat stroke) dhu ga-waynha luku-dhawu malaŋu, ga dhu ga-waynha ga-djämanydja ga-djämanydja guŋga'yunna. Wiripuŋu malaŋu dhu ga-wayŋa galkala, ga dhu ga-waynha guŋga'yunnha. Wañ'marama 000, ga dhu ga-waynha guŋga'yunna djälkthurr-yätj malaŋu nhethunarama.

## Djälkthurr-dharrana

**Baḏatj:** Djambatjtjan-ŋurru, luku-djarraga, ŋurrpu, ŋapa-ŋapayi, waŋarr-djäṯitjnha, ga gululu ga-djänana.

1. Goŋmirri djawuluŋnha, goŋmirri djawuluŋnha, ga dhu ga-waynha djunnguŋnha garwuraŋ'nha.
2. Djawuluŋnha ga ga-waynha djunnguŋnha garwuraŋ'nha.
3. Djunnguŋnha ga ga-waynha djunnguŋnha garwuraŋ'nha, ga dhu ga-waynha ga-djämanydja ga-djämanydja guŋga'yunna.
4. Djunnguŋnha ga ga-waynha djunnguŋnha garwuraŋ'nha, ga dhu ga-waynha ga-djämanydja ga-djämanydja guŋga'yunna.
5. Dhu ga-waynha ga-djämanydja ga-djämanydja guŋga'yunna.
