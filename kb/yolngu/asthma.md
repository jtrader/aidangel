# Gaarrwarwu Guḻku' — 4 x 4 x 4 Waŋa

Manaŋguma dhuwurrnydja, ga gaarrwarna 4 gora gaarrwarwu guḻku'wu gal'yunmiyaŋ’ gaarrwar'ŋu ga walŋakumawu' — 1 gora bili, 4 gora dhuŋgarma' dhuŋgarma' — ga balyama guḻku'wu 4 minitj, balyama bili yaka. Wäkuma 000 yiniy gaarrwar'ŋu dhika yaka nhina baṯthurrnydja.

## Dharaŋana gaarrwarwu guḻku'

- Dhuŋgarma', ganybu walŋa, buku-wubum' rumbalŋuru dharraŋana
- Dharraŋana ḏälnydja waŋana yulŋunydja ga dhä-yurruyarnydja
- Wataŋuy, djamarrkuḻiny' djalkiri, rapiy rapiny' yindi
- Rapiny' djamarrkuḻiny', baḏaŋ'thurrnydja — wäkuma **000 yurruya dhika**

## Gaarrwarwu Yothinyamirr'ku Yirr'yun (4 x 4 x 4)

1. **Manaŋguma dhuwurrnydja.** Dhika yaka gurrwuḻkunydja ga nhina ga rumbalnydja yäku.
2. Gaarrwarna **4 bili gora** gaarrwarwu/wuḻurrwu gaarrwar'ŋuwu ga walŋakumawu'wu (eg. Ventolin, Asmol) — 1 gora bili, ga barrku'yurru gaarrwarna **4 dhuŋgarma'** gal'yunmiyaŋ' gapuŋuru dhuŋgarma' bili. Gaarrwarna gal'yunmiyaŋ' gapuŋuru dhäwu'yurru.
3. **Balyama 4 minitj.**
4. Yiniy yaka baṯthurrnydja, gaarrwarna bili **4 bili gora**.
5. Yiniy bili yaka baṯthurrnydja, **wäkuma 000** ga yuwalkthiya gal'yunmiyaŋ' 4 gora 4 minitj bili gapuŋuru yakanha ambalanhi nhina yäkuyurru.

## Yindi guḻku' — wäkuma 000 gulŋunydja

Wäkuma 000 yurruya dhika yiniy:
- Dhuwurrnydja dhiya-gay'yurru gaarrwarma' dharraŋana.
- Djamarrkuḻiny' rapiny' dharraŋana.
- Dhuwurrnydja baḏaŋ'thurrnydja.
- Yaka gaarrwar'ŋu guḻku' nhina yäkuyurru.

Ambalanhi yäkuyurru dhiya-yaka, yuṯurruya gal'yunmiyaŋ' 4 gora 4 minitj bili.

## Adrenaline (anaphylaxis ga gaarrwarwu)

Yiniy dhuwurrnydja yuwalkthiya nhina EpiPen ga dharaŋana yindi guḻku'gu, gaarrwarna **adrenaline gulŋunydja**, ga balyama gaarrwarwu guḻku'wu walŋakumawu'wu. Nhina [anaphylaxis](/kb/anaphylaxis).
