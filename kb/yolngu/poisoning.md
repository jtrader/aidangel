# Gaṉarrwuy Dhäkay-ngäṉarrwuy

Gaṉarrwuy dhäkay-ngäṉarrwuy malaŋu warray, ga walal nheny walŋakuman goŋmirriyamirriwuy Information Centre ga ŋäŋ'thurran 13 11 26. But nheny yätj ga yothu malaŋu warray, ga walal nheny ŋäŋ'thurran 000. Dhuwalaŋuwuy nheny yaka guḻku' ga ŋuli marrtjiyan, ga ŋal'yunna nhuma guḻku' ga ŋuli yothun.

## Melwuy Dhuwalaŋuwuy

1. Goŋmirriyamirri [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
2. **Walal nheny walŋakuman Goŋmirriyamirriwuy Information Centre ga ŋäŋ'thurran 13 11 26** (24/7, Australia-wiyaŋ'thun).
3. Walal nheny ŋäŋ'thurran **000** but nheny yätj ga, but nheny bunbunna ga, but nheny dhunupa ga, but nheny yothu ga.
4. **Yaka nheny guḻku' ga ŋuli marrtjiyan** but nheny ŋal'yunna ga Goŋmirriyamirriwuy Information Centre.
5. Dhuwalaŋuwuy yakanhany gaṉarrwuy, butjuy, ga dhäkay-ngäṉarrwuy malaŋu warray, ga dhuwalaŋuwuy dhuŋanaŋun.
6. But nheny yätj ga, but nheny bunbunna ga, dhuwalaŋuwuy ['warrkthunana dhäparŋ'thunana' (recovery position)](/kb/recovery-position) ga ŋuli marrtjiyan.

## Nhunana gaṉarrwuy (gas, buku, wata)

- Dhuwalaŋuwuy nheny rirrakay buku-ḻäŋ'thunana malaŋu **but nheny dhunupa ga** dhuwalaŋuwuy.
- Walal nheny ŋäŋ'thurran **000**.
- Dhuwalaŋuwuy nheny malaŋu ŋuli bunbunna ga.
- Dhuwalaŋuwuy nheny CPR ga ŋuli rur'maram.

## Dhuyu gaṉarrwuy (bawala)

- Dhuwalaŋuwuy nheny malaŋu ŋuli barrpuy'yunna ga ŋuliny.
- Bili dhuwalaŋuwuy nheny malaŋu ŋuli buku-ḻäŋ'thunana marrtjiyan bitjanna 20 minitj.
- Walal nheny ŋäŋ'thurran Poisons ga 000.

## Butjuy ŋal'yunan

Ŋal'yunan butjuy malaŋu dhuwalaŋuwuy yindi **walŋakuman goŋmirriyamirriwuy**. Walal nheny ŋäŋ'thurran **000** bitjanna — butjuy malaŋu dhuwalaŋuwuy ŋuli butjuy'yunna ga dhäkay-ngäṉarrwuy bitjanna dhunupa guḻku'.
