# Ŋäthiliŋu Narkul

Ŋäthilimirriŋuwu, gay' nherra' ŋäthi ŋulaŋuru wiripuŋuwuy ga djurana ga wänaŋuru, ga yaka djalkthun ŋulaŋuru ŋäthililili. Biyi'na walal bili ŋäthili ga wäna 000-nha. Ga ŋäthilimirriŋuwu ŋulaŋuru ŋathaŋuru, maḻaw wäna ŋulaŋuru djurana ga wäna 20 miniwumu ga wäna ŋulaŋuru ambulancethe.

## Dhuyu ŋäthiliŋu (gatpa, gukurr)

1. Yaka miriŋuny ŋäthi.
2. Butthun walal ŋäthi walalaŋguny darratjŋura.
3. Nherra' djurana ga wäna, ŋulaŋuru wiripuŋuwuy.
4. Ga yaka dhiyaŋuy, yäkatthun walal ŋäthi ga wäna ŋulaŋuru medical aid-thirri.

## Dhuyu ŋäthiliŋu (garrkuŋuru)

1. Yaka **ŋuli** djalkthun.
2. Yäkatthun walal ŋäthi ga wäna ŋulaŋuru gatjalaŋuyaŋu, ga yaka miriŋuny.
3. Walal **wiripuŋuŋuŋu** ŋäthi ga wäna ŋulaŋuru birrimbirrmirrŋuŋuŋuŋu.
4. **Wäna 000.**

## Ŋathaŋuru ŋäthiliŋu

1. **Gatjalaŋuy** nherra' djurana ga wäna ŋulaŋuru ŋäthilili 20 miniwumu. Butthun walal ŋäthi walalaŋguny darratjŋura ŋulaŋuru ŋathaŋuru ŋäthililiŋuŋuŋuŋuŋu.
2. Dhatthun walal ŋäthiliŋu.
3. **Wäna 000.**
4. Ga ŋuliŋuru ŋäthiliŋu ŋathaŋuru, dhuwal walal dhuyuŋu ŋurruŋuŋuŋu hospital-thirri.
