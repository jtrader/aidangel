# Gaŋga'yun Bathi Gaḻaŋura

Gaŋga'yun Bathi gaḻaŋura rirrakaynydja gaŋga'yuŋu, rirrakaynydja galkinyamirri, ga lili'yunmiya gaŋga'yunmiya baṉḏaḻnydja rom-ŋura, ga balyi'yuŋu wäŋa'lili' yunmiya, ga manikajnydja wukirri'yunmiya yana gali'ŋura ambulance-nydja ga raŋinyamirriya.

## Waṉa'yun Manymak

1. **Telepoŋmirriya 000-lili'** ŋunhi bathinyali' gaḻaŋura. 
2. Yakaŋuru [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
3. Balya gaŋga'yuŋu ga ŋäŋ'kuru'yuŋu ŋunhi bathinyali' (garrku'yuŋu gaŋga'yuŋu yakanhawi gaŋga'na ŋunhi yolŋu walŋa).
4. Gaŋga'yuŋu **lili'yunmiya, galkinyamirri, ga yakaŋuru gaŋga'yuŋu rirrakaynydja** ŋunhi bathinyali' manikajnydja, ŋunhi miyalkkurru wata'ŋura ga ŋunhi wata'ŋura walŋamirri.
5. Gaŋga'yuŋu bathinyali' rirrakaynydja, gaŋga'mirriya ŋunhi rirrakaynydja gaŋga'yuŋu rom-ŋura.
6. Keep pressure on — do **yaka** gali'yuŋu ŋunhi rirrakaynydja ŋunhi manikajnydja.
7. Wukirri'yunmiya [shock](/kb/shock) ga ŋunhi yolŋu walŋa.

## Ḏarramu gaḻaŋura

**Yaka** gali'yuŋu ŋunhi ḏarramu gaḻaŋura bathinyali'ŋura. Gaŋga'yuŋu rirrakaynydja ḏarramu gaḻaŋura ga gaŋga'yuŋu ḏarramu gaḻaŋura ŋunhi bathinyali'ŋura, ga ŋunhi rirrakaynydja galŋaŋura gaŋga'yuŋu.

## Gurruṯu'mirri

- Gaŋga'yuŋu bathinyali' rirrakaynydja.
- Walŋamirriya ŋunhi gurruṯu'mirri ŋunhi walŋamirri wata'ŋura, walŋamirriya ŋunhi miyalkkurru wata'ŋura, ga ŋunhi miyalkkurru wata'ŋura ŋunhi bathinyali'ŋura — **yakaŋuru ŋunhi** bathinyali'ŋura walŋamirriya ga yakaŋuru bathinyali'ŋura.
- Waṉa'yunmiya hospital-lili' ŋunhi yolŋu walŋa.

## Tourniquet

A commercial tourniquet should only be used for life-threatening limb bleeding that cannot be controlled with direct pressure. Apply 5–7 cm above the wound (not over a joint), tighten until bleeding stops and note the time.
