# Djälkiriŋu rom

Gaŋgathinyan romnha rom-djälkiriŋunha waŋganyŋura yolŋuwu, ga guŋga'yurr yuṯun marrtjinyawuy bukumirriyaŋgurr, ga waŋganydhun waṉaŋgurr'kuŋu walalmba gapunta walŋamirriŋurunha. Romdja djälkiriŋu, gumurrŋura rom-dhunupa—nhe gapu-yatjku gaŋgathinyan romnha, ga waŋganydja gapu-yatjku dharpa—dhunupa warraŋuluyuna djorra'wuyyi ga dhunupa ŋatha djälŋurunha romnha walŋamirra romŋura.

## Rom dhäwu

- Yakaŋura romŋura, ga yindi walŋa gapu-yatjku
- Mundhurr yindi djiwarr' ga yatjku rom ga yatjku walŋa gapu
- Dhä-birrka'yun, gumurr-nhäŋal, dhuŋana
- Wataŋuyuna, dharpaŋura romŋura
- Guŋga'yun romŋura ga rom-yatjku (rom-dhunupa)

## Nhä rom warraŋuluyina

1. Gaŋgathinyan romnha rom-djälkiriŋunha waŋganyŋura yolŋuwu, ga guŋga'yurr yuṯun marrtjinyawuy bukumirriyaŋgurr.
2. Yuṯun marrtjinyawuy bukumirriyaŋgurr, ga waŋganydhun waṉaŋgurr'kuŋu walalmba gapunta walŋamirriŋurunha—guŋga'yun romnha ga guŋga'yun romnha—romnha yolŋuwuy romnha walŋamirra.
3. Walŋamirra romnha gapu-yatjku romnha, gapu-yatjku romnha, ga gapu-yatjku romnha.
4. Rom gamurru'yu romŋura ga rom-dharpaŋura romnha—romnha djälkiriŋu ga romnha djälkiriŋu (e.g. Hydralyte).
5. **Goŋ wäŋa 000** rom-dhunupa—nhe gapu-yatjku gaŋgathinyan romnha, ga waŋganydhun waṉaŋgurr'kuŋu walalmba gapunta walŋamirriŋurunha, ga rom-dhunupa romnha romŋura, ga rom-dhunupa romnha romŋura.
