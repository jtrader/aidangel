# Djarraya Bäyŋu — Gaŋaŋatj Yolŋuŋu MiranaŊ

Waŋaŋ bukmak djarraya bäyŋu yakaŋ miyamurra rrambaŋalmirri gaŋaŋa Australiaŋu, gaŋakulŋu dhuŋa 000 gaŋaŋatj Yolŋuŋu miranaŊ balayiŋ nhanukala rrambaŋalŋuŋ bitjan dhuŋa rrambaŋalŋuŋ, gaŋaŋatj bukmak nhanukala walga' gaŋaŋatj manikay mala, gaŋakulŋu dhuŋa gaŋaŋatj wirri balayiŋ nhanukala walga' gaŋaŋatj manikay mala, gaŋakulŋu dhuŋa djaŋam nhanukala walga' gaŋaŋatj manikay mala gaŋaŋatj dhuŋa djuŋaya' mirri nhanukala gaŋaŋatj manikay mala.ŋ.

## Dhuŋa Nhaltjan (dhuŋa waŋana djarraya)

1. **Gaŋakulŋu dhuŋa 000.**
2. Gurruŋu dhuŋa [ŋäkuru DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd).
3. Dhuŋa gaŋaŋatj nhanukala bapurruŋu **wirri yakaŋ bukmak gulkthu** gaŋaŋatj djuŋaya' mirri. Bukmak dhuŋaŋurru dhuŋa miyamurra.
4. Gaŋaŋatj dhuŋa **miranaŊ gaŋaŋatj Yolŋuŋu**:
   - Waŋaŋ dhuŋa waŋa Yolŋuŋu yuulŋu miranaŊ (yakaŋ dhuŋa waŋa Yolŋuŋu bukmak djalkthunŋuru).
   - Waŋaŋ dhuŋa gaŋaŋatj rrambaŋalŋuŋubalaŋ, dhuŋa gaŋaŋatj wirri — bitjan dhuŋa gaŋaŋatj yolŋuŋu djalk. 
   - Gurruŋu dhuŋa **walga' bukmak walga'** , dhuŋa gaŋaŋatj bukmak balayiŋ.
   - Nhani Yolŋuŋu rrambaŋalŋuŋubalaŋ balayiŋ nhanukala Yolŋuŋu dhuŋa rrambaŋalŋuŋubalaŋ.
5. Gaŋaŋatj dhuŋa **wirri** nhanukala walga' dhuŋa djuŋaya' mirri.
6. Nhani dhuŋa waŋaŋ nhanukala bäyŋuŋ gaŋaŋatj Yolŋuŋu.
7. Gurruŋu dhuŋa mäyŋuŋu gaŋaŋatj dhuŋa mäyŋuŋu. Dhuŋa gaŋaŋatj [ŋäkuru CPR](/kb/cpr).

## Dhuŋa Yakaŋ

- Dhuŋa **yakaŋ** dhuŋa dhawaŋatj rrambaŋalŋuŋubalaŋ — Yolŋuŋu dhuŋa waŋaŋ nhanukala rrambaŋalŋuŋu.
- Dhuŋa **yakaŋ** dhuŋa djalkthun yakaŋ rrambaŋalŋuŋu.
- Dhuŋa **yakaŋ** dhuŋa tourniquet.
- Dhuŋa **yakaŋ** dhuŋa rrambaŋalŋuŋu — Yolŋuŋuŋ dhuŋa gaŋaŋatj Yolŋuŋuŋ yakaŋ nhanukala Yolŋuŋu.
- Dhuŋa **yakaŋ** dhuŋa djarraya.
