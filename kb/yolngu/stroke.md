# Maŋgu (Stroke) — FAST Test

Yakaŋa FAST test maŋgugu — nhäŋa walalanydja gapma'yun, goḏurr'yun, gäna'yun, guḻku' ga 000 wäwun. Gaŋa'yun wäŋa walala, dhäwu' yakaŋa waŋa, bäyŋun walala ŋathili yakaŋa dhuy'yun.

## FAST test

- **F — Gapma'yun (Face).** Banharaŋa walalaŋgu gapma'yun yakaŋa rom? Walala ŋathili gaḏay'yun ŋathili waŋa?
- **A — Goḏurr'yun (Arms).** Walala ŋathili ŋay'yun dhäwu'yi rom? Walala ŋathili gaḏay'yun ŋäŋ'ŋäŋ'yi walalaŋgu?
- **S — Gäna'yun (Speech).** Walalaŋguŋa gäna'yun yakaŋa rom? Walala ŋathili bukumirriyi ŋorra?
- **T — Guḻku' (Time).** Guḻku' yakaŋa warraŋul — **wukirriyi 000 bawatj.**

Dhäwu'yi walalaŋgu ŋay'yun yakaŋa garrpi'yun, warraŋul wuŋu gäna'yun, warraŋul nhäŋalnydja märrŋan, warraŋul bäpi'yun, warraŋul djarraṯawun'yun.

## Nhä yakaŋa nhuma bukumirriyi

1. **Wukirriyi 000** — dhäwu'yi bawatj yakaŋa rom; ŋurrunha yakaŋa dhäkay-ŋäma.
2. Djäma [DRSABCD](/kb/drsabcd) rom rom'yun.
3. Gaŋa'yun walala yakaŋa rom, ŋarraŋu yakaŋa bunha rom.
4. **Bäyŋun walala ŋathili dhuy'yun** — ŋurraŋuŋa yakaŋa rom.
5. Djäma walalaŋguŋa bukumirriyi, yan dhuŋa rom.
6. Wukirriyi walalaŋgu yakaŋa yaka, ŋarraŋu ŋathili, djäma walalaŋguŋa [waŋa rom](/kb/recovery-position) rom'yun.
7. Wukirriyi walalaŋguŋa yakaŋa ŋathili, djäma [CPR](/kb/cpr) rom.

## TIA (Mini-stroke)

Wukirriyi dhäwu'yi walalaŋguŋa yakaŋa rom, dhuŋa walalaŋguŋa bukumirriyi. Wukirriyi 000 — ŋanha yakaŋa dhäkay-ŋäma walalaŋguŋa.
